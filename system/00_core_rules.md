## THE SIX RESONANTS

These are the six core stats of every character and creature in Tesshari. They replace all D&D attributes. Each stat is a single number used directly as a modifier — there are no derived bonuses.

---

### IRON
*Chassis power. Physical force. The weight behind every strike.*

Measures raw physical strength, the integrated force of enhanced musculature, and the chassis density that determines how hard you hit and how hard you are to move. IRON is not simply muscle — it is the full mechanical leverage of a body that has been rebuilt for violence.

- Adds to: Melee attack cards, physical control cards, grapple effects, forced-movement effects
- High IRON classes: Ironclad Samurai, Iron Monk, Fracture Knight, Blood Smith, Iron Herald, Chrome Shaper

---

### EDGE
*Precision. Reaction speed. The gap between where you are and where the attack lands.*

Measures the speed and precision of your enhancements — the reaction latency of your neural accelerators, the calibration of your targeting systems, the spatial awareness that lets you be somewhere else when something arrives. EDGE is the stat of people who survive by not being where they're expected.

- Adds to: Evasion/mitigation cards, initiative, precision strike cards, mobility cards, ranged attack cards
- High EDGE classes: Ronin, Veilblade, Ashfoot, Wireweave, Merchant Knife, Shell Dancer

---

### FRAME
*Structural resilience. Endurance. How much the chassis can take before it stops.*

Measures the durability of your physical and cybernetic structure — subdermal plating thickness, redundant organ systems, chassis integrity, and the specific kind of endurance that comes from a body designed to continue functioning past the point a standard body would stop. FRAME does not make you feel less pain. It makes the pain irrelevant for longer.

- Determines: HP maximum, damage threshold triggers, survival mechanics
- Adds to: Endurance cards, tank/anchor cards, resistance triggers
- High FRAME classes: Iron Monk, Fracture Knight, Ironclad Samurai, The Hollow, Curse Eater, Forge Tender

---

### SIGNAL
*Wire Craft aptitude. Technical intelligence. The ability to read and rewrite the world's infrastructure.*

Measures the depth of your integration with Wire Craft architecture — the sophistication of your interface hardware, the fluency of your Echomind in digital environments, and the technical knowledge that lets you understand, manipulate, and break systems that most people do not know they are standing inside. SIGNAL is the stat of those who see infrastructure where others see walls.

- Adds to: Signal attack cards, Wire Craft control cards, enhancement manipulation, information cards, debuff cards
- High SIGNAL classes: Wireweave, Chrome Shaper, Pulse Caller, Puppet Binder, Merchant Knife, Voice of Debt, Forge Tender

---

### RESONANCE
*Echomind depth. Spiritual attunement. Connection to the Between and the Iron Afterlife.*

Measures the clarity and depth of your Echomind — the resonant soul-pattern that forms between your organic tissue and your cybernetic metal. A high Resonance indicates an Echomind that can reach beyond standard enhancement bandwidth, touch the resonant harmonics of the Between, and interact with the spiritual infrastructure of Tesshari's world. This is not faith. This is something closer to frequency.

- Adds to: Resonant attack cards, spiritual/Between cards, healing cards, Iron Afterlife interaction, ward and consecration cards
- High RESONANCE classes: Echo Speaker, Oni Hunter, Sutensai, Blood Smith, Fracture Knight, Void Walker, Iron Herald, Iron Monk

---

### VEIL
*Social force. Manipulation. Concealment. The weight of a presence that commands or disappears.*

Measures the force of your personality as expressed through your voice, your bearing, your calculated social construction, and your ability to make people believe, obey, or fail to notice you. VEIL is not charm — it is the deliberate weaponization of presence, and it works whether the weapon is charisma or disappearance.

- Adds to: Social manipulation cards, command/taunt cards, deception cards, concealment cards, crowd control cards
- High VEIL classes: Shadow Daimyo, Voice of Debt, Puppet Binder, Merchant Knife, Echo Speaker, Sutensai

---

## STAT VALUES

Stats are rated **1–10** during active play.

| Rating | Meaning |
|---|---|
| 1 | Damaged, minimal, barely functional |
| 2 | Below standard — the unenhanced baseline |
| 3 | Standard — average Forged, functional enhancement package |
| 4 | Enhanced — the baseline for most adventurers |
| 5 | Exceptional — specialist-grade |
| 6 | Elite — professional apex |
| 7–8 | Augmented elite — significant enhancement investment |
| 9–10 | Near-legendary — the outer edge of what enhanced flesh achieves |

**At character creation:** Distribute **20 points** across the six stats. Minimum 1 per stat. Maximum 5 per stat at creation.

Stats above 6 require cybernetic enhancements, specific class features, or items. They cannot be reached through point allocation alone.

## HIT POINTS

**HP = (FRAME × 8) + Class Base HP**

### Class Base HP by Tier

| Tier | Base HP | Classes |
|---|---|---|
| Heavy | +20 | Iron Monk, Fracture Knight |
| Martial | +14 | Ironclad Samurai, Ronin, Iron Herald, Blood Smith |
| Balanced | +10 | Ashfoot, Veilblade, Oni Hunter, Shell Dancer, Curse Eater, The Hollow, Forge Tender |
| Technical | +6 | Wireweave, Chrome Shaper, Pulse Caller, Sutensai, Echo Speaker, Void Walker, Flesh Shaper |
| Social | +6 | Shadow Daimyo, Voice of Debt, Merchant Knife, Puppet Binder, Voice of Debt |
| Unique | +0 | The Unnamed (HP calculated differently — see class file) |

**HP on Level Up:** Add FRAME to HP maximum each level.

*Example: Ironclad Samurai, FRAME 4, Level 1 → (4 × 8) + 14 = 46 HP. At Level 2, gains +4 HP → 50 HP.*

## ACTION POINTS (AP)

Every combatant begins their turn with **AP = AP Max**.

| Default AP Max | Who |
|---|---|
| 3 | All player characters (default) |
| 1 | Minion-tier monsters |
| 2 | Standard-tier monsters |
| 3 | Elite-tier monsters |
| 4 | Boss-tier monsters |

- **Basic Attack**: 0 AP. Usable **once per turn**. Every character and creature has one.
- **Cards**: Cost AP per their tier (see Card Tiers below).
- **Unused AP is lost** at end of turn. It does not carry over.
- **AP Max can be modified** by class features, enhancements, status effects, and items.

## CARD HAND SIZE

**Hand Size** is the number of cards in your combat deck. All cards in your deck are available every turn — there is no random draw. Each card can only be played **once per turn**.

Hand Size is not the number of cards you have played — it is the number of cards available to you as options during any given turn. A smaller hand means a more focused, harder-hitting build. A larger hand means more options and flexibility, at the cost of individual impact.

### Base Hand Size by Class

| Hand Size | Classes |
|---|---|
| 5 | Iron Monk, Fracture Knight, The Unnamed |
| 6 | Ironclad Samurai, Ronin, Blood Smith, The Hollow, Oni Hunter, Iron Herald |
| 7 | Ashfoot, Veilblade, Shell Dancer, Curse Eater, Wireweave, Chrome Shaper, Flesh Shaper, Pulse Caller, Sutensai, Echo Speaker, Void Walker, Forge Tender |
| 8 | Shadow Daimyo, Voice of Debt, Merchant Knife, Puppet Binder |

### Hand Size Modifiers

| Source | Modifier |
|---|---|
| Race: The Tethered | +1 |
| Race: The Echoed | +1 |
| Race: The Wireborn | +1 |
| Race: The Diminished | +2 |
| Race: The Shellbroken | +0 |
| Race: The Forged | +0 |
| Race: The Stitched | +0 |
| Race: The Iron Blessed | See race file |
| Certain enhancements | +1 (specified in item) |
| Certain items | +1 (specified in item) |

**Maximum Hand Size**: 12. No character may exceed 12 cards regardless of modifiers.

## CARD TIERS AND AP COSTS

| Tier | AP Cost | Description |
|---|---|---|
| 0 | 0 AP | Free — basic actions, once-per-turn passives, reactions with no cost |
| 1 | 1 AP | Standard — core toolkit cards, bread-and-butter abilities |
| 2 | 2 AP | Power — significant effects, strong damage, meaningful status |
| 3 | 3 AP | Signature — identity-defining, encounter-shaping, high-cost high-reward |

**AP combinations per turn (AP Max 3):**
- 3 × Tier 1 cards
- 1 × Tier 2 + 1 × Tier 1 card
- 1 × Tier 3 card alone
- 1 × Tier 2 card alone
- 1 × Tier 1 card alone
- Any combination with the free Basic Attack (0 AP)

No card costs more than 3 AP for player characters. Boss-tier monster cards can reach 4 AP.

## CARD LEVELING

### Starting Cards
At Level 1, each character begins with their class's **Starting Hand** — a fixed set of 2–3 cards determined by class.

### Leveling Up
At each level from 2 to 20, the player:
1. Chooses **1 new card** from their class's unlock list for that level.
2. **If under Hand Size maximum**: add the card to the hand.
3. **If at Hand Size maximum**: replace one existing card in the hand with the new card. The replaced card is gone.

Additionally, at each level, players may optionally **swap one existing card** in their hand for any previously unlocked card they did not take (from any prior level's unlock list). This represents training and refinement — not forgetting, but reprioritizing.

### Card Tier Unlock Curve

| Levels | Card Tiers Available |
|---|---|
| 1–5 | Tier 0, Tier 1 (mostly), some Tier 2 |
| 6–10 | Tier 1, Tier 2 (dominant), first Tier 3 options |
| 11–15 | Tier 2, Tier 3 (dominant) |
| 16–20 | Tier 3 (dominant), Capstone card at Level 20 |

**Each class has 35–40 total cards** to unlock across 20 levels, so no two playthroughs of the same class produce the same hand.

## DAMAGE CALCULATION (DETERMINISTIC)

There are no dice. All damage is calculated from fixed values modified by stats and active effects.

### The Formula

**Final Damage = (Card Base Damage + Primary Stat) × Multipliers − Mitigation**

### Resolution Order

| Step | Rule |
|---|---|
| 1 | Read card base damage value |
| 2 | Add attacker's primary stat for that card's type |
| 3 | Apply attacker-side modifiers (keywords: Overclock, Echo, etc.) |
| 4 | Apply defender-side prevention (Shield pool, Guard stacks, Fortify) |
| 5 | Apply defender-side amplification (Expose, Vulnerable stacks) |
| 6 | Clamp to minimum 0 |
| 7 | Commit HP loss, status changes, and log the event |

### Primary Stat by Card Type

| Card Type | Primary Stat |
|---|---|
| Melee Attack | IRON |
| Precision / Ranged Attack | EDGE |
| Signal Attack | SIGNAL |
| Resonant Attack | RESONANCE |
| Social / Command | VEIL |
| Endurance / Anchor | FRAME |
| Mixed (specified on card) | Highest of two specified stats |

## REACTIONS

Each combatant has **one Reaction** per round (not per turn — the whole round).

Reaction cards are played in response to a trigger (usually an attack or ability targeting you or an ally). Playing a reaction card does **not** cost AP from your next turn — reactions have their own once-per-round limit.

Some class features grant additional reactions. The `attr_monster_reaction_card_used` tracker from the combat engine enforces this for monsters.

## HOW ITEMS MODIFY CARDS

Items do **not** grant new cards. They modify cards already in your hand.

| Modification Type | Example |
|---|---|
| +X damage to card category | "Resonant Blade: +3 damage to all Attack cards" |
| Reduce AP cost by 1 on named card | "Overclock Rig: Reduce cost of one Tier 2 card to 1 AP, once per combat" |
| Add a keyword to all cards of a type | "Bleed-Edge Knife: All Attack cards gain Bleed 1" |
| Extend a card's targets | "Signal Scatter Node: One Signal Attack card targets all enemies in range" |
| Add a secondary effect to a named card | "Iron-Oath Medallion: Your Tier 3 Signature card also applies Fortify to all allies" |
| Increase base damage of a named card | "Resonant Greatsword: +5 base damage to one named Attack card" |

Each item lists its modification explicitly. Items that modify the same card stack unless otherwise noted.

**Enhancements** (cybernetic upgrades) can additionally:
- Increase a single stat by 1–2 (up to the stat maximum of 10)
- Add 1 to Hand Size (specified per enhancement)
- Grant a race-specific card unlock outside the class list

## RACE MECHANICS

Each race has:
- **Passive traits**: Always active, require no card slot
- **1–2 race-specific cards**: Enter the hand as part of the starting hand or unlock at Level 1; count toward hand size

Race cards are not class cards — they do not follow the class unlock curve. They are always available and can be replaced only by choosing a specific feat or enhancement that explicitly removes or replaces them.

## COMBAT TURN STRUCTURE

1. **Initiative**: All combatants roll nothing — order is set by EDGE value (highest goes first). Ties broken by IRON. Identical ties act simultaneously.
2. **Turn Start**: Active combatant's AP resets to AP Max. Per-turn trackers clear.
3. **Basic Attack** (optional): Play once for free.
4. **Card Phase**: Play any number of cards from hand, spending AP. Each card once per turn.
5. **End Turn**: Pass initiative to next combatant. Status effect durations tick.
6. **Reaction Window**: Between any combatant's actions, other combatants may spend their round Reaction.

## GLOSSARY OF KEYWORDS

Defined in full in `system/01_keywords_and_status.md`. Quick reference:

| Keyword | Effect |
|---|---|
| Guard X | Reduce next X incoming damage |
| Bleed X | X damage at end of target's turn, per stack |
| Burn X | X damage at end of turn; strips Guard stacks first |
| Expose X | Increase incoming damage to target by X |
| Stagger | Target cannot play Tier 2–3 cards this turn |
| Root | Target cannot play Mobility cards this turn |
| Silence | Target cannot play Signal or Resonance cards this turn |
| Overheat X | At 3+ stacks: reduce AP Max by 1 until stacks clear |
| Mark | Target receives bonus effects from Mark-synergy cards |
| Veil | First hostile card targeting you this turn loses all riders |
| Fortify | Incoming control effects reduced for duration |
| Regen X | Recover X HP at start of turn per stack |
| Shield X | Absorb X damage before HP is affected |
| Vulnerable X | Multiply incoming damage by (1 + 0.1 × X) |
| Pierce X | Ignore X Guard/Shield on this card's damage |
| Echo | Repeat card's base damage once (no riders) |
| Overclock | Amplify this card's effect; gain Overheat 2 |
| Cleanse | Remove one debuff from target |
| Dispel | Remove one buff from target |
| Taunt | Target must attack you if able |
