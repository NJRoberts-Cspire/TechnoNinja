## PRIOR ASCENDED ARTIFACTS

---

**The Unnamed Fragment**
Item — Prior Ascended Artifact

A shard of ancient resonant metal resting in an open palm, photographed close, filling most of the frame. The material defies classification — smooth beyond any natural stone, amber light pulsing from within in long, slow intervals like a sleeping heartbeat. The surface is neither rock nor metal nor glass but something before all three, worn to an almost organic roundness by three thousand years of existence. The palm beneath it looks mortal and temporary by contrast. Deep black background swallows everything but the fragment and the hand. The amber glow throws no sharp shadows — it radiates, diffuses, breathes. There is no decoration, no markings. It predates language. Tabletop RPG illustration, FromSoftware artifact photography aesthetic, dark fantasy.

Suggested aspect ratio: 1:1
Tags: ancient artifact, resonant metal, amber glow, close-up, dark fantasy, FromSoftware aesthetic, pre-civilization relic
