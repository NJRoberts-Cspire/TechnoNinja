## KORRATH THE UNBROKEN

---

**Korrath the Unbroken — Portrait**
Character portrait

Close-up portrait of a massive armored figure whose face is a composite of melted-together battlefield masks — enemy generals' visages fused into one surface, layered and wrong, some features still legible beneath the amalgam. The steel does not look manufactured; it looks grown, scabbed over, accreted over decades. Deep seams where flesh once was. No skin visible above the neck. Lower chest and collar visible: patchwork of mismatched metals from different eras, different armies, different defeats. Lighting: single warm source from below-left, casting harsh shadows across the mask-topography. Palette: charcoal, cold steel, dull bronze, old blood. Mood: immense weight, not cruelty — something that has cost everything to become this. Dark fantasy concept art, painterly, cinematic.

Suggested aspect ratio: 2:3
Tags: dark fantasy portrait, cyborg, war trophy, FromSoftware aesthetic, feudal Japan, patchwork armor, melancholic

---

**Korrath the Unbroken — Action Shot**
Action/scene illustration

A figure of enormous scale stands in the immediate aftermath of a fatal blow — the wound still visible across his torso, steel edges slowly drawing together like a mouth closing, the metal reforming as though remembering its shape. His posture is neither triumph nor agony: he is simply still, watching the space where an enemy stood. Debris around him. The closing wound glows faintly at the seams — not magical, biological. His many-masked face is turned slightly downward. Lighting: dramatic overhead with rim light catching the reforming edges. Cold blue-silver environmental light against warm amber wound-seams. Palette: deep blacks, charcoal, steel, amber glow along wound lines. Tone: the silence after violence, neither victory nor grief.

Suggested aspect ratio: 16:9
Tags: dark fantasy action, cyborg regeneration, aftermath, FromSoftware aesthetic, dark tech fantasy, war, cinematic lighting

---

**Korrath the Unbroken — Symbolic**
Symbolic/iconic image

An enormous iron fist, scarred and ancient, the knuckles worn smooth by decades of war. Cradled in the hollow of the palm: a small fish-shaped object cast in resonant metal, warm amber-gold, the surface worn to a shine by handling. The fish is handmade — the edges slightly imperfect, the details loving. It glows softly with the biological warmth of resonant metal. The contrast between scale and delicacy is the entire image. The fist is not gripping — it is holding. Lighting: the fish provides its own glow, the only warm source in the frame. Dark background. Palette: iron black, cold steel, single point of amber-gold warmth. Mood: an enormous thing being careful with something it cannot name.

Suggested aspect ratio: 1:1
Tags: symbolic, resonant metal, dark fantasy, contrast, inheritance, loss, object study

## VAEN OF THE EMPTY HAND

---

**Vaen of the Empty Hand — Portrait**
Character portrait

Portrait of a woman of extreme age — not frail, but distilled. Her face is entirely organic: every enhancement removed over thirty years, the absence visible as faint scars, healed removal sites at the temple and jaw, the eyes bare. The absence is more striking than any augmentation could be. Her expression is neither kind nor unkind; it is absolutely still, the way very deep water is still. Her eyes look forward at something that is not in the frame. Pale robes, undyed, worn through long use. White-silver hair. Lighting: soft and diffuse, no harsh shadows — the stillness extends to the light itself. Palette: white, ivory, pale grey, cool negative space. Mood: the uncanny calm of someone who has ceased to want anything.

Suggested aspect ratio: 2:3
Tags: dark fantasy portrait, organic, elder, absence, calm, unsettling beauty, feudal Japan

---

**Vaen of the Empty Hand — Action Shot**
Action/scene illustration

A vast space — the outskirts of a city, morning light, something terrible recently concluded: bodies at the edges of the frame, smoke, the evidence of her pilgrims' passage. At the center stands one small still figure in pale undyed robes. She is not fleeing. She is not surveying. She is simply present, hands at her sides, watching. She is entirely unharmed. The horror of the image is in her stillness — she has not caused this and she has not stopped it and she seems to experience no contradiction there. Lighting: grey dawn light, diffuse, the smoke catching the early sun. Palette: grey, pale ash, dark aftermath contrasting her white robes. Mood: the most frightening thing in the scene is the one who feels nothing.

Suggested aspect ratio: 16:9
Tags: dark fantasy scene, aftermath, stillness, horror, moral ambiguity, atmospheric, feudal Japan cyberpunk

---

**Vaen of the Empty Hand — Symbolic**
Symbolic/iconic image

A single outstretched hand, palm upward, entirely organic — aged skin, the scars of old removal sites visible at the wrist. The hand is open. The hand holds nothing. The image asks: what does nothing look like when it is the most powerful thing in the scene? The answer: the negative space above the palm has a quality of attention, as though emptiness here is not absence but presence of a different kind. Soft ambient glow from below the hand, as though it is receiving light from an unseen source. Pale, isolated. Palette: warm ivory skin, cool grey background, the single warm undertone beneath. Mood: the weight of a thing that gave everything up and calls it peace.

Suggested aspect ratio: 1:1
Tags: symbolic, empty hand, organic, power, absence, minimalist, dark fantasy

## THE HOLLOW AUTHOR

---

**The Hollow Author — Portrait of Shivenne**
Character portrait

Portrait of a woman in scholarly robes — ink-stained fingers resting on a closed text, the robes of a religious academic, hair simply pinned. She looks like everything a traveling scholar should look like. She is taking notes. She is asking intelligent questions. She is slightly too attentive, listening with a quality of processing rather than presence. Her eyes are sharp and entirely empty of the micro-distraction that living people carry — no internal weather, no wandering thought. Not unintelligent: the opposite. The wrongness is subtle: everything right, nothing present. Warm candlelight. Palette: warm amber candlelight, dark ink-stained robes, the small glint of something in her eyes that shouldn't be there. Mood: the uncanny valley of a perfect performance.

Suggested aspect ratio: 2:3
Tags: dark fantasy portrait, puppet, scholar, uncanny, feudal Japan, subtle horror, warm lighting

**The Hollow Author — Portrait of Lord Tessik**
Character portrait

Portrait of a lord in formal military dress — the kind of face everyone trusts, warm eyes, the slight comfortable weight of a man who is easy in rooms. He is smiling at something off-frame, genuinely pleased, the laugh lines convincing. He has been beloved for thirty-one years. Look at him long enough: something is not timing correctly. The warmth arrives a beat before the stimulus. The eyes are slightly too still between expressions, the way a very good actor rests between lines. He is not threatening. He is charming. The horror is that you can almost not see it. Palette: formal northern military dress — grey and muted blue, silver buttons, firelight. Mood: the warmth of a man who is not there.

Suggested aspect ratio: 2:3
Tags: dark fantasy portrait, puppet, lord, uncanny, warm lighting, subtle horror, political figure

**The Hollow Author — The Truth**
Scene illustration

A single person alone in a small room. The room is filled with interface surfaces — displays of some kind, ambient and numerous, showing faces: dozens of them, each a different person's eyes, expression, context. The walls glow with them. The figure at the center is small. They have not spoken to another human in eleven years. They are dressed in plain cloth that has not been thought about in a long time. Their posture is the posture of someone who exists only when performing the others: at rest, they seem almost not to be there. One hand rests on a surface. Their face, seen from a distance, is neither cruel nor mad — it is the face of someone for whom the world has contracted to this room and these faces and nothing else. Lighting: cold blue-white from the interfaces, no natural light, a single lamp turned very low. Palette: cold blue-white, warm amber lamp glow, deep black surrounding. Mood: a god behind the curtain who is also a person who is also alone.

Suggested aspect ratio: 16:9
Tags: dark fantasy scene, puppet master, isolation, horror, cold light, control, deeply human
