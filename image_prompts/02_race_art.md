# 1. THE FORGED

**The Forged — Clan Mon**
Race Logo / Icon

A circular mon-style badge, samurai clan crest fused with circuit board. Central motif: a stylized human figure whose ribcage opens to reveal layered metal plates, like armor that is also anatomy. The body silhouette is bold and clean. Outer ring is a thick band of interlocking hexagonal cells suggesting both chain mail and processor nodes. Thin circuit traces radiate from the figure like meridian lines. All rendered in flat metallic tones — deep gunmetal, aged brass, one thin ring of amber glow at the outermost edge. Crisp vector-style illustration with subtle engraving texture. Badge worn at the edges, as if cast from old iron. Black background.

Suggested aspect ratio: 1:1
Tags: clan mon, circuit board crest, metallic badge, flat icon, dark fantasy insignia, samurai heraldry, game UI icon

---

**The Forged — Portrait (Ironhold Variant)**
Race Portrait / Half Body

An Ironhold Forged soldier, half-body portrait. Massive frame. The left arm is entirely metal — not armored, but the arm itself, plates following musculature as if the flesh beneath simply became steel over decades. Salvaged war metal, pitted and scarred. The face is still human, jaw strong, expression weathered, but the right cheekbone has cracked open to show a lattice of steel beneath — healed over in places, almost scar tissue made of metal. Wearing layered do-maru armor that is continuous with the body's own plating. Amber faintly pulses in deep joint seams. Dramatic underlighting from a furnace source. Deep charcoal background. Dark Souls concept art style, photorealistic brushwork.

Suggested aspect ratio: 2:3
Tags: cyborg samurai, FromSoftware concept art, dark fantasy portrait, integrated metal flesh, feudal Japan cyberpunk, dramatic lighting, tabletop RPG character

---

**The Forged — Concept Sheet (Three Kingdoms)**
Race Concept Sheet / Multiple Figures

A concept art sheet showing three Forged individuals side by side, labeled by region. Left: Ironhold — bulky salvaged plating, asymmetric war-scarred metal, heavy and brutal, layers of mismatched steel welded to the body over generations. Center: Wire Market — slim and precise, chrome surfaces machined to mirror smoothness, enhancements symmetrical and intentional, a merchant-caste aesthetic of clean lines and embedded data ports at temple and wrist. Right: Ashlands — jury-rigged survival, copper wire wrapped around a forearm replacement, a lens jury-mounted over one eye, mismatched but functional, a scrapper's ingenuity. All three wear kimono-derived garments worked around their metal anatomy. Neutral grey paper texture. Annotation lines but no text. Clinical and beautiful.

Suggested aspect ratio: 16:9
Tags: character lineup, concept sheet, three variants, dark fantasy cyborg, feudal Japan, tabletop RPG races, design reference

# 2. THE TETHERED

**The Tethered — Clan Mon**
Race Logo / Icon

A circular mon-style badge. Central motif: a single unadorned human hand, palm open, fingers together — no metal, no ports, no circuitry. Deliberately plain against the complex world around it. The hand is surrounded by a fine web of circuit traces that stop just short of touching it, as if the network acknowledges its absence. Outer ring is plain brushed wood-grain texture rather than metal, suggesting organic material in an inorganic world. Rendered in warm ivory, pale skin tones, and deep brown. The only color accent is the glow of the circuits that do NOT touch the hand — their amber light illuminating it from outside. Engraving style, aged but careful.

Suggested aspect ratio: 1:1
Tags: clan mon, organic icon, dark fantasy insignia, absence of technology, samurai heraldry, game UI icon, negative space design

---

**The Tethered — Portrait**
Race Portrait / Half Body

A Tethered woman, half-body portrait. Entirely unmodified, every inch of her conspicuously flesh. In a world of integrated metal, she looks startlingly fragile and vibrantly alive — skin that has color and warmth no chrome can replicate, hands that are just hands, eyes that are just eyes. She wears formal furisode layered in dark indigo and charcoal, embroidered with wave patterns, no circuits sewn in, no data ports at the collar. She stands very still. Her gaze is direct and ancient-feeling. Soft warm candlelight from one side, cold ambient blue from the other — the world's technology reflected on her without touching her. Dark background. The portrait captures both vulnerability and an odd dignity. Dark Souls art style.

Suggested aspect ratio: 2:3
Tags: organic human, feudal Japan costume, dark fantasy portrait, tabletop RPG race, melancholic lighting, beautiful vulnerability, cyberpunk contrast

---

**The Tethered — Concept Sheet**
Race Concept Sheet / Multiple Figures

A concept art sheet showing four Tethered individuals: a wandering monk in road-worn travel robes carrying a wooden staff, an elderly healer with cloth wraps on his hands and a worn medicine kit, a young child in plain kimono whose youth makes her unmodified state seem natural but whose wide eyes suggest she already knows she is different, and a Tethered soldier — the most striking figure, wearing traditional lamellar armor made entirely of lacquered wood and leather, carrying a yumi bow, standing among the faint afterimage silhouettes of armored Forged who dwarf him in scale. All on neutral paper with faint aged-parchment tone. The sheet communicates: these people are wholly human, and that is both beautiful and dangerous.

Suggested aspect ratio: 16:9
Tags: character lineup, unmodified human, feudal Japan aesthetics, dark fantasy, tabletop RPG race sheet, organic contrast, concept reference

# 3. THE ECHOED

**The Echoed — Clan Mon**
Race Logo / Icon

A circular mon-style badge. Central motif: a human figure rendered twice — once in solid ink, once in a faint ghost-line offset slightly to the right, as if lagging a half-second behind. The two figures share the same center but the ghost-line version is thinner, paler, lit from within by amber. Outer ring composed of alternating solid and broken circuit segments — continuity interrupted and resumed. Background of the badge is near-black with the faintest amber gradient bloom at center, like a coal still warm. The whole thing feels like a memory of itself. Metallic ink style, engraved but delicate.

Suggested aspect ratio: 1:1
Tags: clan mon, ghost icon, dual silhouette, amber glow, dark fantasy insignia, game UI icon, digital resurrection symbol

---

**The Echoed — Portrait**
Race Portrait / Half Body

An Echoed man, half-body portrait. He looks almost entirely human — almost. The skin has a faint amber luminescence beneath it, as if his veins run with low light rather than blood, most visible at the temples, throat, and the backs of the hands. His eyes glow very softly, amber-warm, and hold a slight unfocus — he is present but also elsewhere, receiving a signal from a world most cannot perceive. He wears the layered kosode of a scholar or advisor, dark grey silk with fine amber thread work at the collar. The expression is gentle, searching, and sad. Single candle-source lighting from below, casting deep shadows above. A faint translucency to the edges of his form as if the light passes through him too easily.

Suggested aspect ratio: 2:3
Tags: digital ghost, amber bioluminescence, dark fantasy portrait, translucent figure, feudal Japan clothing, tabletop RPG, haunted beauty

---

**The Echoed — Concept Sheet**
Race Concept Sheet / Multiple Figures + States

Figure two: a decades-old Echoed, glow settled deep under the skin, barely visible in daylight, controlled and calm but the eyes still hold the distance. Figure three: an Echoed in a moment of strong emotion — the glow blooms outward involuntarily, skin going semi-translucent, the ghost-impression of the digital self visible for a moment as a secondary outline. Lower panel: a detail study of the hands showing the amber vein patterns under the skin. Dark paper ground. Annotation lines only, no text.

# 4. THE WIREBORN

**The Wireborn — Clan Mon**
Race Logo / Icon

A circular mon-style badge. Central motif: a small human figure sitting cross-legged, but rendered as if partially composed of fine circuit traces rather than solid flesh — the lower half of the body dissolves into flowing data lines that curl back inward like calligraphy brushstrokes. Around the figure, concentric rings of data: thin lines of scrolling information at microscale, illegible but clearly structured. The outer badge ring is a dense band of binary-adjacent marks, like a data barcode wrapped into a circle. Color: pale silver-white on near-black, with one ring of cold ice-blue rather than amber. The feel is delicate, fast, and slightly alien.

Suggested aspect ratio: 1:1
Tags: clan mon, data figure, circuit calligraphy, cold blue glow, dark fantasy insignia, game UI icon, network being

---

**The Wireborn — Portrait**
Race Portrait / Half Body

A Wireborn woman, half-body portrait. Small and slight, pale skin so thin the bioluminescent circuit-like patterns beneath are clearly visible — not circuitry grafted on, but biological structures that mimic it, glowing a cold blue-white under the skin of the forearms, collar, and temples. Her eyes are extraordinary: irises replaced by shifting data — text too small to read, but moving, responding. She wears a fitted haori of deep black silk, simple cut. Her expression is alert in a way that feels non-human, as if she processes more information per second than she lets on. Three-quarter composition. Cold blue rim lighting from one side, very slight warm amber ambient from the other. Background: server-room dark, faint data-light.

Suggested aspect ratio: 2:3
Tags: wireborn portrait, bioluminescent circuits, data eyes, cold blue glow, dark fantasy, feudal Japan cyberpunk, tabletop RPG race

**The Wireborn — Concept Sheet (Split State)**
Race Concept Sheet / States + Detail

A concept art sheet. Left: a Wireborn man in his grounded state — compact, alert, bioluminescent patterns warm and contained. Center: the same man in the SPLIT state — his body is present but dimming, eyes gone blank white as his consciousness extends elsewhere. Right: a detail panel showing four variations of the under-skin circuit patterns — one that reads like kanji, one geometric, one organic-neural, one grid-like. # 5. THE STITCHED

**The Stitched — Clan Mon**
Race Logo / Icon

A circular mon-style badge. Central motif: a human silhouette divided into clear quadrants by healed seam lines — thick scar-like divisions that run through the figure like the joins in kintsugi pottery, each segment filled with slightly different tones and textures suggesting different origins. The seams are filled with amber gold, deliberately. This is the key visual language: the seams are not hidden, they are the design. Outer ring: a series of interlocking arc segments, each slightly different from its neighbor but forming a coherent whole. The badge reads as assembled but unified. Kintsugi aesthetics applied to a clan crest. Rich gold and aged dark metal tones.

Suggested aspect ratio: 1:1
Tags: clan mon, kintsugi design, seam lines, gold join, dark fantasy insignia, assembled identity, game UI icon

---

**The Stitched — Portrait**
Race Portrait / Half Body

A Stitched man, half-body portrait. A figure assembled from multiple sources, worn and lived-in now — this is years past the joining. His left arm is a darker skin tone than his right, meeting at a well-healed seam at the shoulder. One eye is brown, one eye is amber-lit, suggesting the eye replacement came from an Echoed. His jaw is strongly built but meets his neck at a slightly different angle than born anatomy would allow, a subtle wrongness that reads once you see it. He carries himself with complete confidence. He wears a layered kimono in dark burgundy and black, cut to show the shoulder seam deliberately. The expression is direct, challenging. Rim lighting from above. Background smoke and shadow. Dark Souls portraiture style.

Suggested aspect ratio: 2:3
Tags: assembled body, kintsugi scars, multiple origins, dark fantasy portrait, tabletop RPG, quiet menace, cyberpunk feudal Japan

---

**The Stitched — Concept Sheet**
Race Concept Sheet / Multiple Figures + Detail

A concept art sheet. Three full figures: left — a recently Stitched individual, joins still slightly raised and pink, the seams still clearly medical, body language uncertain; center — an elder Stitched, decades old, the joins faded to pale silver, the body completely inhabited, the stance of someone who has fully owned what they are; right — a Stitched warrior, four visibly different skin tone panels making up the torso and arms, one forearm entirely metal from an unknown donor, tattoos spanning three different skin tones as intentional art that claims all of it. # 6. THE SHELLBROKEN

**The Shellbroken — Clan Mon**
Race Logo / Icon

A circular mon-style badge. Central motif: a face rendered in clean, almost mask-like simplicity — features present but without expression, as if cast from porcelain. Then: a single crack running from the left eye downward, and through that crack, the faintest hint of something underneath — not blood, not circuitry, but warmth. The suggestion of a person becoming. Outer ring: smooth and unornamented on the upper half, gradually fragmenting into crack patterns on the lower half — the badge itself is breaking open. Color: white and pale grey for the face, deep black background, that single amber thread along the crack. Minimalist and unsettling.

Suggested aspect ratio: 1:1
Tags: clan mon, mask crack, emerging identity, minimalist icon, dark fantasy insignia, game UI icon, white and amber

---

**The Shellbroken — Portrait**
Race Portrait / Half Body

A Shellbroken woman, half-body portrait. Her face is the subject. Beautiful in a blank, architectural way — regular features, maintained hair, clean clothing. Everything is correct. And yet: the eyes are wrong. Not in a mechanical way, but in a human way — they hold no expression yet, or rather they hold the very first stirrings of one, a dawning confusion or wonder, like someone who has just heard music for the first time and doesn't have a word for what they feel. She moves fluidly, too fluidly, posture perfect, hands still in a way no relaxed person's hands are still. She wears a clean, well-kept kosode in pale grey-white. The lighting is flat and even, which makes it colder than shadow would. Something is beginning in her face. The portrait should sit with that threshold moment.

Suggested aspect ratio: 2:3
Tags: blank face, awakening identity, uncanny valley portrait, dark fantasy, tabletop RPG, still hands, liminal expression

---

**The Shellbroken — Concept Sheet (Awakening Stages)**
Race Concept Sheet / Expression + Posture Study

A concept art sheet focused on face and bearing across five stages of awakening. Stage one: complete blankness — a puppet-master's instrument, face serene and empty, posture erect and economical. Stage two: confusion, the first crack — a slight furrow between brows, eyes searching, posture fractionally uncertain for the first time. Stage three: emotion arriving, unidentified — face crumpling slightly as something floods in, hands lifting instinctively, the body responding before the mind can name it. Stage four: controlled emergence — a person learning to hold feeling carefully, choosing an expression deliberately. Stage five: partial self — still obviously Shellbroken in the unusual clarity of feature, but something behind the eyes that is unmistakably there. Each stage shown as a tight portrait oval. Dark grey ground. The sheet is a document of becoming.

Suggested aspect ratio: 16:9
Tags: expression study, awakening stages, identity formation, uncanny beauty, dark fantasy concept sheet, tabletop RPG race, five stages

# 7. THE IRON BLESSED

**The Iron Blessed — Clan Mon**
Race Logo / Icon

A circular mon-style badge. Central motif: a human silhouette from which resonant metal formations grow outward — spines and crystalline plates radiating like a sun behind the figure, but irregular, organic, as coral or bone rather than manufactured. The formations glow amber from within. The figure at the center is still, bearing the growth with stillness. The outer ring of the badge is itself composed of angular crystal forms. Color palette: deep black, the warm amber glow of the resonant formations dominant, gold plate at the outer ring edge. The aesthetic sits between a religious icon and a natural history illustration. Sacred and strange.

Suggested aspect ratio: 1:1
Tags: clan mon, resonant metal growth, crystal formations, amber glow, sacred icon, dark fantasy insignia, game UI icon

---

**The Iron Blessed — Portrait**
Race Portrait / Half Body

An Iron Blessed figure, half-body portrait. The resonant metal formations are undeniable — crystalline spines growing from the left shoulder and clavicle, a plate emerging from the jaw and cheek, formations erupting up through the skin of the forearm like coral through seafloor. They glow amber warmly, beautiful and terrible. The skin around the formations is tight and slightly purpled with the permanent strain. The face is serene in the way of someone who has accepted irrevocable things — not peace exactly, but a kind of distance from pain. The eyes are clear and the bearing is dignified. They wear a ceremonial shiromuku-style robe in white now marked with amber staining from the formations. Single overhead lighting, the amber glow from the formations acting as a secondary source. Background is dark temple stone.

Suggested aspect ratio: 2:3
Tags: resonant metal growth, crystalline formations, amber bioluminescence, dark fantasy portrait, sacred pain, tabletop RPG race, isolated dignity

# 8. THE DIMINISHED

**The Diminished — Clan Mon**
Race Logo / Icon

A circular mon-style badge. Central motif: a pair of hands — small, precise, one holding a fine instrument (a jeweler's tool or a miniature probe), the other steady beneath. The hands are detailed at a higher resolution than the rest of the badge — the intricate joints visible, the enhancements minute and beautiful, tiny amber nodes at each knuckle rather than the large plating of the Forged. Outer ring: a fine gear-work pattern composed of extremely small interlocking parts, each one rendered with precision, the overall ring dense with micro-detail that rewards close inspection. The badge rewards closeness — what looked simple from far away is elaborate up close. That is the point. Gunmetal, fine gold, amber accent.

Suggested aspect ratio: 1:1
Tags: clan mon, precision hands, micro detail, fine gear ring, dark fantasy insignia, game UI icon, intricate craftsmanship

---

**The Diminished — Portrait**
Race Portrait / Half Body

A Diminished engineer, half-body portrait. Small frame — the portrait's composition makes clear this figure is three and a half feet tall through posture and the scale of objects in the frame: a tool rack behind them sized for larger people hangs out of comfortable reach. The hands are the portrait's focal point — dexterous, enhancements a marvel of miniaturization, tiny amber nodes at each knuckle, fine wire-work traced through the metacarpals, a lens mounted at the end of one finger. Sharp eyes that miss nothing. The face is angular and alert, a small scar through one brow from work in a tight space. They wear a craftsman's shortened haori with many precisely arranged pockets, each pocket containing specific tools at specific angles. Expression: the calm certainty of someone who is better at their job than anyone in the room. Lighting from a work lamp below and to the left.

Suggested aspect ratio: 2:3
Tags: small stature portrait, precision hands, master craftsman, dark fantasy, tabletop RPG race, intricate micro enhancements, feudal Japan workshop

---

**The Diminished — Concept Sheet**
Race Concept Sheet / Scale + Detail

A concept art sheet built around scale and mastery. Center: a Diminished figure shown in full at accurate scale beside two full-size figures (one Forged, one Tethered) — the size difference stark and matter-of-fact. The Diminished figure's posture is relaxed, expert, unbothered. Left panel: an extreme close-up of a Diminished hand working on a component too delicate for larger fingers — the precision enhancement work shown in full detail, tiny amber nodes, fine engraved tracery at the finger joints, a lens mounted at the nail-bed. Right panel: four Diminished shown in different roles — a wire-runner navigating a maintenance crawlspace, a surgeon, a clockwork armorer, a scout in tight terrain. All are competent and specific. No condescension in the framing — this is a capability sheet, not a pity sheet. Clean grey-paper ground with amber accent lines.
