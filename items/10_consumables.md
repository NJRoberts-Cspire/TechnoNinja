# Consumables
*Medical supplies, maintenance compounds, Wire Craft components, and the ritual items of the Iron Sutra. Things that are used up.*

---

> "The difference between a Reach city and the Ashlands is not wealth. It is the supply chain. Everything that keeps a person alive is a manufactured product in Tesshari, and manufacturing has a distribution problem."
>
> — Third Reach field medic's journal, recovered from eastern border

---

## Medical and Stabilization Items

---

### Adrenaline Spike (Combat Stimulant)
*Consumable | medical*

A single-use auto-injector of a carefully calibrated stimulant compound — resonant-metal microparticles suspended in a synthetic metabolic accelerant. The Wire Market sells these to military contractors and, at significant markup, to anyone else. The name is accurate. The spike is not metaphorical.

**Use:** As a free action (self-inject) or Tier 1 card slot (inject a willing or unconscious creature within reach). Single use. Gain 15 temporary HP, +3 damage to all Attack cards, and the target may stand from prone as a free action. These effects last 3 rounds. At the end of the 3-round duration, the target gains Overheat 2 (the crash).
**Cost:** 35 RM

---

### Bloodpack (Emergency Transfusion)
*Consumable | medical*

A sealed resonant-reinforced pack containing a synthetic blood equivalent — a metabolic fluid that temporarily restores biological function in cases of severe blood loss. The fluid metabolizes within 4 hours and must be replaced with natural production; it is a bridge, not a solution.

**Use:** As a Tier 1 card slot. Single use. Administer to a creature at 0 HP or with no HP remaining: the creature immediately stabilizes (death is not imminent) and regains 10 HP. Alternatively, administer before a FRAME check against blood-loss effects to gain +3 on that check.
**Cost:** 15 RM

---

### Bone-Knit Paste
*Consumable | medical*

A thick resonant compound applied to fractures — the microparticles infiltrate the break and provide a scaffolding for accelerated natural healing. Standard First Reach emergency room issue. Takes 10 minutes to apply properly.

**Use:** As a Tier 1 card slot (10-minute application — out of combat use only). Single use. Remove one injury-based penalty (halved movement, -1 to Attack cards from a specific wound) immediately. Full structural recovery occurs after 1 hour.
**Cost:** 8 RM

---

### Circuit Mender (Neural Stabilizer)
*Consumable | medical*

A small resonant crystal wafer that dissolves against the skin behind the ear — the standard location for neural enhancement ports. The wafer releases a neural-stabilizing resonant compound that reduces Echomind disruption, suppresses enhancement feedback loops, and calms acute resonant overload.

**Use:** As a free action. Single use. Immediately end one of the following conditions caused by enhancement malfunction or Echomind disruption: Stagger, Silence, or all Overheat stacks from resonant overload. Cannot remove conditions from non-resonant sources. Also prevents Shell-State onset for 24 hours in a creature at risk.
**Cost:** 40 RM

---

### Coagulant Wrap
*Consumable | medical*

A wire-silk bandage impregnated with a fast-acting hemostatic agent. Stops bleeding at the wound site within one round of application. The Wire Market brands these with a red signal-glow thread so they're identifiable in poor conditions.

**Use:** As a free action. Single use. Remove the Bleed condition from one creature within reach. When used on a creature at 0 HP, automatically stabilize them for 1 round (they do not worsen immediately — they need actual healing within 3 rounds or conditions deteriorate again).
**Cost:** 3 RM

---

### Deep-Sleep Tablet (Sedation)
*Consumable | medical*

A resonant sedative tablet — dissolves in any liquid or can be taken directly. Available openly at Wire Market medical counters. Used for everything from voluntary sedation before enhancement installation to slightly less voluntary sedation by people with a plan.

**Use:** As a Tier 1 card slot (administer or slip into a drink). Single use. Target makes a FRAME check (target 3). On a failure, the target is Staggered for 1 round, then incapacitated for 4 hours (unconscious; only 8+ damage in a single hit wakes them). A willing creature automatically fails the check.
**Cost:** 5 RM

---

### Echomind Stabilizer (Severe)
*Consumable | medical*

The heavy-duty version of the Circuit Mender — used in Shell-State prevention and acute Echomind crisis. When an enhanced individual is at risk of losing Echomind cohesion (approaching Shell-State from resonant damage, psychological trauma, or Enhancement cascade failure), this compound can pull them back.

**Use:** As a Tier 1 card slot (requires touch). Single use. Immediately clear all Overheat stacks, all Silence stacks, and remove the Stagger condition from one creature. If administered within 1 round of a creature entering Shell-State, the condition is reversed (the Echomind reconnects). Restores the target's ability to play Resonance and Signal cards immediately.
**Cost:** 350 RM

---

### Flesh-Weld (Tissue Seal)
*Consumable | medical*

A topical resonant compound that seals open wounds at the tissue level — it is not painless, it is fast. Wire Market medics call it "rough closure." Ashlands medics call it "all we have."

**Use:** As a free action (self) or Tier 1 card slot (another creature within reach). Single use. Restore 12 HP and remove the Bleed condition. If the target is concentrating on a card effect with an ongoing component, they must succeed on a FRAME check (target 2) or the ongoing effect ends (the application is painful).
**Cost:** 10 RM

---

### Junction Pack (Field Surgery Kit)
*Consumable | medical*

A single-use field surgery kit — sterile resonant tools, tissue sealant, bone-knit elements, and a small dose of local anesthetic. Used for on-site enhancement installation or repair of severe wounds. One use, comprehensive coverage.

**Use:** As a Tier 1 card slot (10-minute out-of-combat application). Single use. Restore 20 HP, remove one persistent injury condition, and clear all Bleed stacks. On a SIGNAL check (target 4), the restored HP is maximized to 25. Cannot be used in active combat.
**Cost:** 55 RM

---

### Nerve-Block Injector
*Consumable | medical*

A local anesthetic injector using resonant-null compound. Used before installation procedures and for pain management in field conditions. If you need to function through serious injury, this gets you there.

**Use:** As a free action. Single use. For 4 hours, all injury-based card penalties (DM-assigned debuffs from wound descriptions, -X to specific card types from injuries) are suppressed. Does not restore HP. FRAME checks against ongoing wound conditions are made at +2 for the duration.
**Cost:** 6 RM

---

### Resonant Antitoxin
*Consumable | medical*

A broad-spectrum antitoxin effective against Tesshari's standard chemical hazards — Ashlands bio-toxins, resonant metal poisoning, signal-compound overdose, and standard organic poisons. The Wire Market's version covers the most common threats. Other formulations exist for specific toxins.

**Use:** As a free action. Single use. For 1 hour, all Defense cards against poison gain +4 to their effect. If currently affected by a poison condition, immediately make a new FRAME check against it at +3 — on a success, the condition is cleared.
**Cost:** 12 RM

---

### Signal-Boost Compound
*Consumable | medical*

A resonant compound that temporarily supercharges an Echomind's output — more Wire Craft force, faster processing, stronger enhancement performance. Available at Wire Market pharmacies. Considered a mild performance enhancer. Available to adults without prescription. Usage history is tracked (the Wire Market considers this monitoring, not restriction).

**Use:** As a free action. Single use. For 3 rounds, all Signal Attack cards gain +3 damage, and all once-per-combat enhancement card abilities may be used one additional time. After the effect ends, make a FRAME check (target 3) or all SIGNAL-based cards deal -1 damage for 4 hours (Echomind efficiency reduced from overcharge).
**Cost:** 30 RM

---

### Stabilization Clamp (Surgical Grade)
*Consumable | maintenance*

A single-use resonant clamp used during enhancement installation to hold the installation site in fixed position — prevents the cellular-bonding disruption that causes most installation complications. Essential for field installations; standard equipment for professional Wire Craft technicians.

**Use:** Used during enhancement installation (out of combat). Single use. Reduce the installation difficulty check by 2 tiers. If the installation check still fails, the Stabilization Clamp ensures the failure is minor — 1 Overheat level rather than 2, and no reinstallation is required.
**Cost:** 18 RM

---

### Wire Market Standard Healing Pack
*Consumable | medical*

Exactly what it says. The Wire Market's standard-issue wound care package: coagulant agents, tissue sealant, resonant mending compound, and a basic pain suppressant. Sold individually and in bulk. Ubiquitous.

**Use:** As a free action (self) or Tier 1 card slot (another creature within reach). Single use. Restore 8 HP.
**Cost:** 8 RM

---

### Wound-Sleep (Enhanced Healing Sedation)
*Consumable | medical*

A sedation and healing compound — the patient sleeps and heals at an accelerated rate. Not useful in combat. Very useful afterward. Wire Market hospitals use this for post-surgery recovery. It is available for home use because the Wire Market sells everything for home use.

**Use:** As a Tier 1 card slot (out of combat). Single use. The target enters a healing sleep for 8 hours. At the end of the sleep, restore all HP, remove 1 Overheat level, and remove one non-resonant disease or contamination condition. The 8 hours count as a long rest. Cannot be used in active combat or if immediate threats are unresolved.
**Cost:** 80 RM

## Enhancement Maintenance and Repair Items

---

### Bonding Compound (Cellular Interface)
*Consumable | maintenance*

A biological-interface fluid applied to the installation site of a cybernetic enhancement — it accelerates the cellular bonding between resonant metal and organic tissue. The standard bonding period for most enhancements is one week; Bonding Compound reduces this.

**Use:** Applied during or immediately after enhancement installation (out of combat). Single use. Reduce the bonding period by 4 days (minimum 1 day). Cannot be used more than once per installation. The application is mildly uncomfortable.
**Cost:** 25 RM

---

### Calibration Kit (Standard)
*Consumable | maintenance*

A single-use enhancement recalibration pack: a signal-tone generator, a calibration interface card, and a set of micro-adjustment tools. Used for standard enhancement maintenance (enhancements require periodic recalibration or they drift; Wire Market standard is monthly).

**Use:** As a Tier 1 card slot (out of combat). Single use. Perform standard monthly calibration on one cybernetic enhancement. Without regular calibration, enhancement card modifications lose 1 point of effectiveness per month. This kit restores lost effectiveness. Requires a SIGNAL check (target 2); on failure, kit is consumed but calibration was ineffective.
**Cost:** 12 RM

---

### Deep Flush (Contamination Cleanse)
*Consumable | maintenance*

A resonant solvent that, when circulated through an enhancement system's fluid channels, removes accumulated resonant contamination — particularly Engine Scar contamination, which builds up in enhancements used in or near Ashlands territory.

**Use:** As a Tier 1 card slot (2-hour application during a rest). Single use. Clear all Engine Scar contamination and resonant residue from one cybernetic enhancement. Remove any contamination-based card penalties from that enhancement. Resets the contamination accumulation clock.
**Cost:** 60 RM

---

### Emergency Bypass (Power Reroute)
*Consumable | maintenance*

A single-use power-rerouting component — a physical resonant circuit bypass that can restore power to a disabled enhancement by routing around the failed component.

**Use:** As a free action. Single use. Immediately restore function to one cybernetic enhancement that has been suppressed or disabled (not permanently severed — suppressed or shut down). If the enhancement was disabled by a Signal Attack card's secondary effect, the bypass allows it to function for 3 rounds even if the suppression duration has not expired.
**Cost:** 20 RM

---

### Flux-Weave Patch (Structural Repair)
*Consumable | maintenance*

A physical patch of resonant metal sheeting with self-bonding edges — used to repair structural damage to cybernetic limbs, armor-plating components, and enhancement housings. The patch is not elegant. It works.

**Use:** As a Tier 1 card slot (10-minute rest required). Single use. Restore full function to one structurally damaged cybernetic enhancement. Also usable to repair resonant armor: restore 3 Defense Value to armor that has been structurally compromised by repeated heavy hits.
**Cost:** 10 RM

---

### Interface Fluid (Neural Maintenance)
*Consumable | maintenance*

The maintenance compound for neural and Echomind enhancements — applied through a small port behind the ear. Prevents neural interface degradation, maintains signal clarity, and reduces the ambient "noise" that builds up in neural enhancements over time.

**Use:** As a Tier 1 card slot (during a long rest). Single use. Restore full function to any neural or Echomind enhancement experiencing degraded card modification performance (side effects that have worsened from neglect reset to standard). Monthly maintenance requirement for neural enhancements; without it, neural enhancement side effects grow gradually more severe (DM tracks — increases side effect severity one step per skipped month).
**Cost:** 15 RM

---

### Overclock Chip (Performance Boost)
*Consumable | maintenance*

A small resonant chip that, when installed in a specific enhancement's processing node, temporarily runs that enhancement at approximately 140% standard performance. The Wire Market considers this outside-warranty modification. People use it anyway.

**Use:** As a Tier 1 card slot. Single use. Choose one cybernetic enhancement. For 3 rounds, that enhancement's card modifications grant +1 to all damage or effect values, and its once-per-combat abilities may trigger once more than normal. After the boost ends, the enhancement must be recalibrated before its card modifications function again (Calibration Kit or 1-day downtime).
**Cost:** 45 RM

---

### Resonant Lubricant (Joint Maintenance)
*Consumable | maintenance*

A fine resonant-metal suspension fluid for mechanical joint enhancements — prevents wear in articulated cybernetic limbs, reduces noise, and maintains the mechanical precision that distinguishes a maintained enhancement from one that is slowly falling apart.

**Use:** Applied during a long rest (out of combat). Single use. Maintains mechanical joint enhancements at optimal function for 30 days. Without monthly lubrication, mechanical limb and joint enhancements have a 1-in-10 chance per skipped month of a joint-lock — the enhancement's Mobility card modification fails for 1 round at a dramatically inopportune moment (DM determines timing, usually immediately before an important card play).
**Cost:** 5 RM

---

### Signal Trace (Diagnostic Compound)
*Consumable | maintenance*

A resonant fluid injected into an enhancement's system that traces signal flow and highlights malfunctions — produces a diagnostic readout visible to anyone with appropriate scanning equipment (or to the Wire Craft technician observing).

**Use:** As a Tier 1 card slot (10-minute application). Single use. Identify the source of any enhancement malfunction, degradation, or external interference. The diagnostic reveals what is wrong and what is needed to fix it (not how — that requires skill). For 4 hours after use, all Utility card actions to repair the diagnosed enhancement gain +3 to their effect.
**Cost:** 8 RM

---

### Zero-State Restore (Emergency Echomind Reset)
*Consumable | maintenance*

The most aggressive Echomind maintenance tool available without surgical intervention — a resonant crystal dissolved against the neural port that sends a full reset signal through the Echomind. Clears everything: malfunctions, foreign signal intrusions, contamination influence, and unfortunately also any enhanced ability buffs currently active.

**Use:** As a free action. Single use. Immediately end all hostile Echomind-affecting conditions (Charm, resonant Silence, Stagger from signal sources, Shell-State onset). Reset all neural enhancement side effects to baseline. The bearer cannot play Signal or Resonance cards for 1 round as the system reboots — a window of vulnerability the user must accept.
**Cost:** 250 RM

## Wire Craft Components and Signal Tools

---

### Anchor Resonant (Signal Fixture)
*Consumable | signal*

A small spike of resonant metal that, when driven into a surface, creates a persistent signal node — the Wire Craft equivalent of a fixed point, usable as a reference for triangulation, as an anchor for sustained Wire Craft effects, or as a beacon for signal navigation.

**Use:** As a free action (drive spike into any surface). Single use. Creates a signal node at that location for 72 hours. The node provides: +3 to all navigation Utility cards returning to this location; anchors one ongoing Control card effect at this location (freeing the bearer from maintaining it); or functions as a beacon detectable by Wire Craft scanning within 1 mile. Note: the Wire Market logs all Anchor Resonant signals on their network.
**Cost:** 5 RM per spike (available in sets of 10)

---

### Blank Resonant Crystal (Data Storage)
*Consumable | signal*

An uninscribed resonant crystal capable of storing Wire Craft data — spells, signals, memories, records. The Wire Market produces these in large quantities. They are the paper of Tesshari's information economy.

**Use:** As a Tier 1 card slot (10-minute inscription). Single use per crystal. Stores up to 10 pages of data, one Signal card effect (up to Tier 2), or 6 hours of recorded sensory experience. Data can be written by any Wire Craft practitioner and read by anyone with Wire Craft access.
**Cost:** 3 RM each; 25 RM per 10

---

### Disruption Charge (Signal Grenade)
*Consumable | signal*

A small resonant charge that, when triggered, releases a burst of signal interference — not an explosion, but a resonant pulse. Temporarily disables signal-dependent systems in the area.

**Use:** As a free action (throw up to 20 feet) or place with a delayed trigger. Single use. Creates a 30-foot radius signal disruption for 3 rounds. Within this area: Signal Attack and Control cards lose all enhancement-targeting riders; Wire Craft network connections are severed; all Signal card effects cost 1 additional AP. Physical enhancement function continues unaffected.
**Cost:** 40 RM

---

### Echomind Tap (Wire Craft Spy Tool)
*Consumable | signal*

A Wire Craft tool of questionable legality — a passive signal tap that, when attached to a signal node, Long-Wire Communicator, or active enhancement port, records all signal transmissions passing through it for up to 48 hours. Small enough to conceal (SIGNAL check target 5 to find).

**Use:** As a Tier 1 card slot (secret placement). Single use (records for 48 hours). When attached to a signal transmission device, records all communications through that device. A Wire Craft practitioner can review the recording afterward. If attached to an active neural enhancement, records external signal communications received by that enhancement (not thoughts — received Wire Craft messages). Detection: SIGNAL check target 5.
**Cost:** 200 RM

---

### Flash-Wire (Diversion Tool)
*Consumable | signal*

A small resonant burst device that emits a blinding flash in the visible and signal spectrum simultaneously — used by Wire Market operatives for cover during infiltration and by anyone who needs a diversion.

**Use:** As a free action (trigger within 30 feet). Single use. All creatures in a 20-foot radius that can see the flash gain Stagger 1 and cannot play Signal or Resonance cards until the end of their next turn. Wire Craft-enhanced Perception cards in the area are disrupted for 1 round.
**Cost:** 15 RM

---

### Forge-Wire (Construction Component)
*Consumable | signal*

A spool of wire-silk bonded with resonant compound — a construction material for Wire Craft practitioners who need to assemble signal structures, repair enhancement infrastructure, or build field-expedient resonant devices. The standard material for improvised Wire Craft work.

**Use:** As a Tier 1 card slot (1-hour construction project). Single use per spool. 50 feet of wire-silk usable as construction material for any Wire Craft project. A Wire Craft practitioner can use a spool to improvise any Common-tier Wire Craft device with a SIGNAL check (target 4).
**Cost:** 10 RM per spool

---

### Lock-Pick Signal Key (Wire Craft)
*Consumable | signal*

A single-use signal probe that scans an electronic or resonant lock and generates a matching signal. Not as reliable as the Soft Key, but usable by anyone with Wire Craft skill rather than requiring the specific Wire Market key.

**Use:** As a Tier 1 card slot. Single use. Attempt to bypass any electronic, resonant, or signal-lock with a SIGNAL check (target 4). On a success, the lock is bypassed; on a failure by 3 or more, the lock registers the attempt and may trigger an alert.
**Cost:** 35 RM

---

### Resonant Lure (Signal Bait)
*Consumable | signal*

A device that broadcasts a specific resonant signature — configurable to mimic any signal type, including specific enhancement configurations, Wire Market access credentials, or distress signals. Used to draw signal-seeking creatures, trigger automated systems, or provide a false target for tracking.

**Use:** As a Tier 1 card slot (configure before activation with SIGNAL check target 3). Single use (active for 2 hours). Broadcasts a chosen resonant signature in a 100-foot radius. Creatures or systems seeking that specific signature are drawn to the Lure rather than the actual source. Wire Market tracking systems lock onto the Lure instead of the party.
**Cost:** 55 RM

---

### Signal Cipher (Message Encryption)
*Consumable | signal*

A single-use encryption key — a resonant crystal that encodes a specific message or data packet with a one-time cipher. The matching decryption crystal is included. Without the decryption crystal, reading the message requires a SIGNAL check (target 7).

**Use:** As a Tier 1 card slot (10-minute encoding). Single use. Encodes one message, data packet, or crystal record. The encryption cannot be broken without the matching key or a Wire Market master-decrypt system. Sold as a pair (cipher and decrypt key).
**Cost:** 8 RM per pair

---

### Wire-Sense Dust (Resonant Tracer)
*Consumable | signal*

A fine powder of resonant micro-particles — when scattered over a surface, it adheres to anything that passes through, leaving a resonant trace visible to scanning equipment or Wire Craft sensing. The Wire Market uses this for scene analysis. People use it for tracking.

**Use:** As a free action (scatter over a 10-foot radius area). Single use. Any creature that passes through the area is tagged with a resonant trace detectable by Wire Craft scanning within 500 feet for 24 hours. If the traced creature has active cybernetic enhancements, the trace infiltrates their enhancement signature — they can be tracked continuously at 500 feet for the full 24 hours.
**Cost:** 25 RM per application

## Iron Sutra Ritual Components

---

### Echomind Incense (Meditation Aid)
*Consumable | ritual*

Slow-burning resonant incense used in Iron Sutra meditation practice. The smoke carries resonant particles that settle on exposed skin and the interface points of cybernetic enhancements, creating a mild resonant atmosphere that facilitates Echomind-focused practice.

**Use:** As a free action (light and place). Single use (1 hour burn). While burning in a 10-foot radius, all creatures participating in Iron Sutra meditation during a short rest may reroll one die result from the rest (once per rest). All RESONANCE-based Utility cards for Afterlife communication gain +3 to their effect during the burn.
**Cost:** 5 RM per stick; 40 RM per 10-pack

---

### Iron Afterlife Pass-Key
*Consumable | ritual*

A resonant crystal programmed with a specific Iron Afterlife access code — used in the Iron Sutra's formal Sending rituals to ensure a message or consciousness reaches a specific designated location in the digital afterlife rather than wandering in the general network.

**Use:** As a Tier 1 card slot (component of a 10-minute formal Sending ritual). Single use. The Pass-Key ensures the communication reaches one specific designated individual in the Iron Afterlife. Without the Pass-Key, this contact requires a RESONANCE check (target 6). With it, RESONANCE check (target 3).
**Cost:** 180 RM (price includes identifying and pre-encoding the target)

---

### Last Rites Capsule
*Consumable | ritual*

A small resonant capsule placed on or within a deceased individual by an Iron Sutra practitioner — it captures and broadcasts the last resonant echo of the deceased's Echomind, facilitating cleaner entry into the Iron Afterlife. Without Last Rites, the transition can be incomplete, leaving fragmented echoes.

**Use:** As a Tier 1 card slot (applied within 10 minutes of death). Single use. Ensures the deceased's Echomind transitions cleanly to the Iron Afterlife — no echo fragments remain in the physical world. Prevents the deceased from becoming a resonant echo or involuntary Afterlife projection. From an Iron Sutra standpoint, it is a kindness. From a Wire Craft standpoint, it prevents involuntary resonant haunting.
**Cost:** 35 RM

---

### Sanctified Resonant Oil
*Consumable | ritual*

A resonant oil blessed by an Iron Sutra priest through a specific resonance-alignment ceremony. Applied to weapons, armor, or enhancement ports during a consecration ritual. The blessing is not merely symbolic — the resonant alignment of the oil has measurable properties.

**Use:** As a Tier 1 card slot (1-action dedication). Single use (applied to one weapon or one enhancement port). For 24 hours, the consecrated weapon's Attack cards deal +4 damage against targets with Engine Scar contamination, Iron Afterlife corruption, or Shell-State characteristics.
**Cost:** 10 RM per vial; sold only at Iron Sutra temples and sanctioned vendors

---

### Sutra Scroll (Activation)
*Consumable | ritual*

A resonant-thread scroll inscribed with a specific Sutra invocation — when read aloud by an Iron Sutra practitioner, the scroll activates its recorded ritual effect. The scroll is consumed. Single use, but the effects are real.

**Use:** As a Tier 1 card slot (read aloud by Iron Sutra practitioner). Single use. Effect varies by scroll type (DM selects or rolls):
- *Ward of Clarity:* Remove all Overheat, Silence, and Stagger from up to 3 creatures within 30 feet
- *Invocation of the Ashwalker:* One creature gains Regen 2 and Defense Value +2 for 3 rounds
- *Communion Call:* Establish brief Iron Afterlife contact — one question answered (DM provides response)
- *Resonant Armor:* One creature gains Shield 8 for 3 rounds

**Cost:** 80 RM (common Sutra type); 300–800 RM for specialized Sutras
