# Cybernetic Enhancements
*Notable pieces — not standard modifications, but items with histories and particular properties.*

---

> "There is no such thing as a neutral enhancement. Every piece of metal you bond to your body was made by someone with an opinion, used by someone with a history, and carries whatever resonance those things left behind. Standard modifications are built to minimize this. The interesting ones are not."
>
> — Senior Wire Craft Technician Asha-Vorn, installation lecture, Wire Market Facility Four

---

## A Note on Enhancement Installation

Standard cybernetic enhancements require a skilled installer (a Wire Craft technician or equivalent), downtime proportional to the enhancement's complexity, and a recovery period. The enhancements listed here are non-standard — unique, historically significant, or sufficiently unusual that installation carries additional considerations.

Unless otherwise noted:
- **Installation Time:** 8 hours (surgical, requiring a SIGNAL check, difficulty 4, by the installer; failure means the installation requires 16 hours and the recipient suffers 2 levels of exhaustion)
- **Recovery:** 3 days of light activity only
- **Removal:** Possible but costly (same installation time, recipient takes 9 damage from cellular disruption)
- **Resonant Bonding:** Most notable enhancements require 1 week of bonding before their unique properties activate

### Widow's Architecture
*Cybernetic Enhancement | Full Forearm Replacement | Very Rare*

A full forearm replacement — from elbow to fingertip — in a dark resonant alloy that catches light differently depending on the angle, giving the arm a quality of depth rather than surface. At rest, it appears as a high-quality limb replacement. The integration point at the elbow is seamless; the cellular bonding is exceptional work.

When activated, the forearm reconfigures: the hand folds back, the dorsal surface of the forearm opens along three seams, and three resonant blades extend — not uniformly, but at the specific angles calculated for maximum efficiency in close quarters. The blades are fixed-angle, not articulated. They are always at exactly the right angle. This is by design: the calculation is built in.

The system was named "Widow's Architecture" by the second person who used it, after the builder's wife, who was killed in the Second Reach's infrastructure wars. The original builder is dead. The name stayed.

**Stat Bonus:** +1 IRON
**Card Modification:** All Melee Attack cards gain Pierce 2. Once per turn, one additional Melee Attack card may be played at no AP cost (the system's blade geometry enables simultaneous strike angles — this replaces, not adds to, any existing multi-attack feature).
**Card Modification:** Opportunity Attack cards played with this system deal +6 damage.
**Side Effect:** Once per long rest, the bearer may activate full extension — all three blades at maximum, angled for a single devastating strike. This Melee Attack card gains Pierce 3 and applies Stagger 1. The target must succeed on a FRAME check (difficulty 4) or lose their next Reaction.
**Integration Cost:** Requires full forearm amputation or replacement of an existing prosthetic. SIGNAL check difficulty 5 for installer (difficulty 6 for a standard technician without structural-combat specialty). 5-day recovery, no exceptions. Integration with the bearer's nervous system completes automatically within 1 week. The system cannot integrate with a bearer who has never been in genuine mortal danger. The architecture recognizes readiness.

*Built by a weapons artificer named Toro-En in the early years of the Second Reach's internal wars. Three were made. The first was destroyed in combat. The third was lost in the Ashlands twenty years ago. This one is held by a retired soldier in the Fourth Reach who has been trying to sell it for three years and keeps refusing every offer — she named it, and she killed her wife's killer with it, and the two things are connected in her.*

**Where Found:** Retired soldier, Fourth Reach. Negotiation required. The price is not the obstacle.

### The Convergence Engine
*Cybernetic Enhancement | Shoulder and Upper Arm Replacement | Rare*

A shoulder-and-upper-arm replacement that integrates a pulse-arm discharge system into the deltoid structure. Compact enough to be concealed under standard clothing with loose outer garments. The discharge aperture is on the palm — a wire-mesh ring with a hollow center that channels the pulse discharge through the natural axis of an outstretched arm.

The system has an audible charge cycle — a high frequency that builds for two seconds before discharge. Anyone who has heard it once recognizes it immediately. This is a feature, not a flaw: experienced users create tactical situations where the sound does work before the shot does.

**Stat Bonus:** +1 EDGE
**Card Modification:** All Ranged Attack cards using this system deal +4 damage and have a range of 30/90 feet. Force damage type.
**Card Modification:** Once per short rest, the bearer may activate Overcharge — all creatures in a 15-foot cone take the card's damage. Targets must succeed on an IRON check (difficulty 4) or be pushed back 10 feet and gain Root 1. After Overcharge, the bearer gains Overheat 2.
**Card Modification:** Ranged Attack cards using this system deal double damage to creatures with more than half their body replaced by cybernetic enhancements (anti-armor design feature).
**Card Modification:** Reduce all incoming force damage by 3.
**Side Effect:** The metabolic drain is constant. The bearer must consume twice the normal amount of food and water to prevent exhaustion buildup. In field conditions without reliable supplies, this is significant.
**Integration Cost:** Shoulder and upper arm replacement. The system draws from the bearer's metabolic process to recharge the capacitor over 6 hours of normal activity. SIGNAL check difficulty 4 for installer; failure causes the capacitor array to bond improperly and must be reinstalled (adding 3 days and one additional level of exhaustion). 7,000 RM.

*Used by militia commander Seta-Orai during the Twelve-Day Siege of the Crescent Gate, where she fired more than two hundred overcharge discharges in twelve days. This unit carries her metabolic signature still faintly embedded in the capacitor calibration. A bearer who integrates occasionally feels, in moments of extremity, a bone-deep tiredness that is not their own — and a bone-deep determination that is not their own either.*

**Where Found:** Wire Market outlet, Second Reach, military-grade used equipment. Priced high but priced correctly.

### Hollow Strike Array
*Cybernetic Enhancement | Full Leg System (Both Legs) | Very Rare*

A full leg replacement from the hip — both legs in standard configuration, though single-leg versions exist at lower cost. The legs are four centimeters longer than biological standard — not enough to look aberrant, enough to matter in movement. The musculature is wire-silk over resonant-metal bone structure, with a strike-surface embedded in the heel and ball of each foot.

At rest: high-quality cybernetic replacements. Under movement: completely silent. The strike surfaces absorb the sound of footfall entirely.

**Stat Bonus:** +2 EDGE
**Hand Size:** +1
**Card Modification:** All Mobility cards gain +15 feet of effective range.
**Card Modification:** Mobility cards played through occupied spaces: any creature the bearer passes within 5 feet of must succeed on an EDGE check (difficulty 4) or take 9 bludgeoning damage and gain Root 1.
**Card Modification — Kinetic Conversion:** When the bearer falls, they may convert all fall damage to a burst Melee Attack card targeting one creature within 10 feet of landing. The card's base damage equals the fall damage that would have been taken; the bearer takes none.
**Side Effect:** The bearer moves silently — not with a bonus to Stealth, silently. No footfall sound is produced. Armor, equipment, and other noise sources still apply.
**Integration Cost:** Hip socket modification and dual leg replacement. Major surgery: SIGNAL check difficulty 5, 8-hour procedure, 7-day recovery minimum. Structural-systems specialty required. The hip socket modification is permanent. 12,000 RM.

*Designed for deep infiltration and kinetic-entry breach work. Full production run of twelve units. Three deployed operatives went missing within six months — not killed, not captured. The program lead filed a report using the phrase "self-directed operational divergence." All three missing operatives left their last known location in the direction of the Ashlands.*

**Where Found:** Wire Market civilian sale, First Reach outlet. Listed as surplus. Complete documentation included. The missing operatives are not in the listing.

### The Patient Eye
*Cybernetic Enhancement | Optical System (Single Eye) | Rare*

An eye replacement — single eye, right side in all known installations. The iris is a fixed, pale grey. The eye does not blink at normal frequency — it blinks when necessary. It is assessing.

Standard visual enhancements (low-light, magnification) plus a predictive analysis layer that calculates probable positions, trajectories, and actions based on observed motion patterns. It does not give perfect foresight. It gives very good guesses, at high speed, from a great deal of data.

**Stat Bonus:** +1 EDGE
**Card Modification:** After observing a creature for one full round, all Attack cards targeting that creature gain +2 damage for the remainder of the encounter. The eye tracks movement patterns.
**Card Modification:** Once per short rest, one Ranged Attack card automatically hits without a check (cannot be a critical). Pure trajectory calculation: guaranteed but unspectacular.
**Side Effect:** The bearer cannot be surprised if their eye is unobstructed. Low-light vision to 120 feet; standard vision magnification up to 20x.
**Integration Cost:** Standard optical installation. No specialty required. 3,500 RM.

*Originally a surveillance tool. This unit contains approximately 8,000 hours of locked read-only surveillance footage from the previous user, who sold the eye quickly and cheaply and has not been findable since.*

**Where Found:** Black market auction circuit, Third Reach. Priced below market because of the data concern.

### Threadwalker Interface
*Cybernetic Enhancement | Neural Spine System | Very Rare*

A spinal column augmentation — not a replacement but an addition, installed along the posterior cervical spine as a series of resonant metal nodes connected by wire-silk conduit. Visible as a slight ridge along the back of the neck, typically concealed under clothing. Under resonant-light conditions, the conduit glows faintly gold.

Interfaces the bearer's Echomind directly with the Wire Craft network — not through external devices but through direct neural signal.

**Stat Bonus:** +2 SIGNAL
**Card Modification:** All Signal Attack cards gain +2 damage when the bearer is directly interfaced with an active Wire Craft network node.
**Card Modification:** Once per short rest, the bearer may concentrate for one minute on a specific resonant signal source within 60 feet. They read the surface content of the most recent transmission — the equivalent of an intercepted spoken message. This requires full concentration (the bearer cannot play cards while doing this).
**Card Modification — Deep Interface:** Once per long rest, the bearer may enter full network immersion for up to 10 minutes. During this time, they are unconscious in the physical world but fully present in the Wire Craft network — able to move through it, access unsecured nodes, and observe network traffic. Their physical body is vulnerable during this time.
**Side Effect:** The bearer can sense the presence of active Wire Craft signals within 300 feet (direction and intensity, not content). They can access public Wire Craft channels without hardware. In environments with heavy Wire Craft traffic, all RESONANCE-based Perception cards take -2 as the constant signal competes.
**Integration Cost:** Cervical spinal modification. High-risk procedure: SIGNAL check difficulty 6, with failure meaning permanent -1 EDGE (minimum 1). Requires Wire Craft specialty in neural interface calibration — a standard surgeon cannot perform this correctly. 15,000 RM.

*An operative named Keth-Vaon disappeared into the Wire Craft deep-network fourteen months ago and has not emerged. Her signal is still present in the deep network, navigating, going somewhere. Her physical body died after thirty-six hours without care. This unit was removed from her body after her death. The Wire Market's ethics board raised concerns. The ethics board was overruled.*

**Where Found:** Wire Market direct sale, high clearance required.

### Cartographer's Sense
*Cybernetic Enhancement | Proprioceptive System | Uncommon*

A distributed system: small resonant nodes installed in the palms of both hands, both feet, and the base of the skull. Subcutaneous — invisible, detectable only as slight warmth to a careful touch. Integrates with the bearer's proprioceptive nervous system, giving a constant awareness of exact position in three-dimensional space.

No dramatic visual component. People who have it often describe feeling, for the first time, exactly where they are.

**Stat Bonus:** +1 EDGE
**Card Modification:** All Mobility cards and Athletics-based cards gain +2 to their effect.
**Card Modification:** All Defense cards against being knocked prone or displaced gain Pierce 2 (they cannot be reduced by standard knockdown effects).
**Side Effect:** The bearer always knows their exact cardinal direction and altitude. They cannot become lost by mundane means. After 1 hour in a new environment, they can reproduce a precise map of it from memory, accurate to within 2 feet.
**Integration Cost:** Multi-site subcutaneous installation. No specialty required. SIGNAL check difficulty 3. 1,800 RM.

*Made by a salvage artificer in the Third Reach named Nen-Dao who was tired of watching people get lost in the Ashlands approaches. He sells them at a fair price, has a waiting list, works slowly and carefully, and removes anyone who asks him to rush from the list. His clients accept this because his work is worth the wait.*

**Where Found:** Nen-Dao, Third Reach, Holding 7. Waiting list. No rushing.

### The Between-Sense
*Cybernetic Enhancement | Echomind Resonator | Very Rare*

An installation at the base of the skull and both temples — three nodes of a rare resonant alloy described in Iron Sutra texts as "the metal that remembers the threshold." Visible as small raised points under the skin, dark against flesh, warm to external touch. They pulse, very faintly, in a rhythm that does not match the bearer's heartbeat.

The sensation of wearing the Between-Sense is described by users as feeling like standing in a doorway — always. Not unpleasantly. Just present at a threshold.

**Stat Bonus:** +2 RESONANCE
**Card Modification:** All RESONANCE-based cards gain +2 when interacting with Iron Afterlife entities or effects.
**Card Modification — Between-Speaking:** Once per long rest, the bearer may attempt to communicate with an Echomind that has been dead for less than 1 year. This requires a RESONANCE check (difficulty 4) and one minute of focus. On a success, a brief exchange (equivalent to five questions and answers) is possible. On a failure, the between-layer responds with static — a disorienting flood of partial signals causing 9 RESONANCE damage.
**Side Effect:** The bearer can perceive the presence of the Iron Afterlife's near-plane as a pressure or presence (not visual). They sense when the dead are nearby. In areas where people have recently died in large numbers — battlefields, hospital districts, Ashlands border zones — all RESONANCE-based Perception cards take -2 as the near-plane noise drowns out the physical world.
**Integration Cost:** Requires an Iron Sutra-trained installation practitioner — this is a spiritual procedure as much as a physical one. The installing priest must perform a Spiritual Reading and 4 hours of ritual calibration before the physical installation. Installation: 2 hours. Recovery: 3 days. Available through Iron Sutra channels; non-members need significant donation, personal connection, or compelling reason. 8,000 RM.

*Developed by the Iron Sutra's second-generation scholars as a spiritual research tool. Forty were ever made. This unit was installed in a child whose parents wanted them to be a priest. The child grew up, became a priest, left the order at thirty-five, and sold the enhancement. They described always being in the doorway as something that made them very good at their work and very bad at their life. They are now a salvage trader in the Sixth Reach and occasionally miss it, in the way you miss a door you used to know the way through.*

**Where Found:** Iron Sutra installation practitioners, with lead time and relationship.

### Afterlife Anchor
*Cybernetic Enhancement | Resonant Heart Casing | Rare*

A resonant metal casing installed around the biological heart — not replacing it, reinforcing it. The procedure coats the heart in a resonant mesh that bonds at the cellular level. From the outside: a slight luminescence visible through thin skin over the sternum, in the register of pale resonant light. The bearer's heartbeat, through resonant-attuned equipment, produces a secondary signal in a register that should not be physically possible from a biological heart.

The casing creates a literal anchor for the bearer's Echomind. The soul is not prevented from leaving the body. But leaving is harder.

**Stat Bonus:** +1 FRAME
**Card Modification:** Reduce all incoming necrotic-type damage by 3.
**Card Modification — Death Resistance:** Once per encounter, when the bearer would be reduced to 0 HP, they may make a FRAME check (difficulty 4). On a success, they are reduced to 1 HP instead. The Anchor holds.
**Card Modification:** The bearer cannot be possessed, cannot have their Echomind displaced, and is immune to any card effect that removes their soul or Echomind from their body involuntarily. The casing is literal — the soul is anchored.
**Side Effect:** Iron Afterlife card effects targeting the bearer require the source to succeed on a RESONANCE check (difficulty 4) or fail entirely. The Anchor is a strong signal and resists interference. However, the casing also makes the Iron Afterlife harder to perceive: all RESONANCE-based cards used to detect, communicate with, or understand Iron Afterlife phenomena take -2.
**Integration Cost:** Open-chest procedure. Failure rate approximately 12% even with a skilled surgeon. SIGNAL check difficulty 6; failure result (roll 1d6): 1-2 bearer takes 22 piercing damage and requires stabilization; 3-4 procedure fails, must be re-attempted; 5-6 heart is damaged and bearer takes -1 FRAME until Wire Craft healing is applied. Recovery: 14 days, bed rest for the first 5. 5,000 RM.

*Field-developed during the Third Convergence War by Wire Craft surgeon Oru-Tesshai, who improvised resonant mesh onto dying soldiers' hearts because the alternative was watching their souls get removed while their bodies kept fighting. Her solution worked. She died in the same battle. Her name is in a footnote in a military engineering manual. The Iron Sutra holds a small annual observance in her honor that they have not told the military about.*

**Where Found:** Wire Craft surgical facilities. Uncommon but not rare. The price is substantial and the risk is substantial.

### The Listening Wire
*Cybernetic Enhancement | Echomind Amplification Array | Very Rare*

A series of wire-silk filaments installed through the cervical spine, terminating in sensor nodes distributed across the skull. Subcutaneous and largely invisible; close inspection of the back of the neck reveals faint seams. The effect is felt rather than seen: the bearer's Echomind resonance broadens in range and sensitivity, able to perceive the spiritual weight of objects, places, and entities that have resonant history.

Described as suddenly being able to hear in a room where you were previously deaf — not a new sense added on top of existing ones, but the realization that the existing sense was operating at a fraction of its capacity.

**Stat Bonus:** +2 RESONANCE
**Card Modification:** All RESONANCE-based Utility cards involving spiritual or historical information gain +3 to their effect.
**Card Modification — Spirit Resonance:** All VEIL cards used in interaction with Iron Afterlife entities or creatures with significant spiritual power gain +2. The entity recognizes the Listening Wire and treats the bearer as spiritually literate.
**Card Modification:** Once per long rest, the bearer may press both palms to a surface and concentrate for one minute. They receive the emotional history of the most significant events that occurred in that location, going back up to 100 years, as sensory impression. The DM provides this.
**Side Effect:** The bearer senses the resonant history of any object they touch — impressions of intensity and emotional weight (not language). A RESONANCE check (difficulty 3 for general sense, difficulty 5 for specific information) translates sensation into useful knowledge. Objects with violent or sorrowful histories are difficult to handle. All fine-manipulation cards take -2 in environments the bearer has not had approximately 10 minutes to acclimate to.
**Integration Cost:** Cervical and cranial installation. Requires an Iron Sutra practitioner with both surgical and spiritual training — two separate checks required: SIGNAL difficulty 5 and RESONANCE difficulty 4 (both from the installer, or two different installers). Installing without proper Echomind mapping has resulted in bearers whose resonance became too broad to filter, leaving them in a state of permanent spiritual overwhelm. Recovery: 5 days. Available only through specialized Iron Sutra practitioners. 12,000 RM.

**Where Found:** Iron Sutra installation practitioners only. Not available through standard Wire Market channels. A non-Iron Sutra applicant needs either a relationship with a senior-rank priest or access to the black market spiritual-enhancement circuit.

### The Second Voice
*Cybernetic Enhancement | Vocal System | Rare | Cursed*

*"The voice it gives you is worth everything. The voice it also gives you is not yours, and it will say things in your sleep."*

A throat and vocal apparatus enhancement — a resonant metal insert that integrates with the larynx and resonance chambers, augmenting the bearer's voice with additional harmonic registers. The enhancement makes the bearer's voice extraordinary: richer, clearer, capable of frequencies that standard biological voices cannot produce.

The installation leaves a slight visible seam along the neck. The voice is immediately noticeable. People look toward the bearer when they speak. They listen without meaning to.

The problem is the second voice.

**Stat Bonus:** +2 VEIL
**Card Modification:** All VEIL-based cards gain +2 to their effect when involving spoken communication.
**Card Modification:** Once per short rest, the bearer may produce a resonant tone affecting all creatures within 30 feet. All targets must succeed on a RESONANCE check (difficulty 4) or have their emotional baseline shifted as the bearer intends (this functions as a Tier 1 Control card applying a chosen emotional state). Creatures can act against this shifted state but must overcome it actively each turn.
**Card Modification:** Once per long rest, the bearer may project their voice at structural intensity — a 10-foot cone dealing 18 thunder damage. All creatures within 30 feet must succeed on a FRAME check (difficulty 5) or gain Silence for 1 hour (their own voice becomes inaudible, not their hearing).
**Side Effect — The Second Voice:** During sleep, the bearer speaks. The second voice uses the enhanced vocal range to produce speech, and the speech is informative. What it says is the bearer's deepest, most unguarded thoughts — the things the bearer would not say consciously. Anyone within hearing range during a long rest hears this speech. The bearer does not wake and does not remember what was said. The speech covers topics the bearer is actively avoiding. Whatever they are suppressing, the Second Voice finds it. Long-term bearers develop strategies: sleeping alone, sleeping with noise to mask it, exhausting themselves so completely that sleep is too short for the Voice to work. None of these strategies are reliable.
**Integration Cost:** Throat installation. SIGNAL check difficulty 3, 4 hours, 3-day recovery. The accessibility is part of the problem. 2,500 RM.

*Designed by a Wire Craft artist interested in the relationship between voice and authenticity. The Second Voice effect was documented in early testing. The designer released it anyway, considering it a feature: "The voice you want and the voice you have. Both of them yours." There are approximately sixty in circulation. Bearers tend to know each other, in a loose network that shares sleeping strategies and processes the social damage from public revelation of private feelings. It is a small community and a strange one but genuine.*

**Where Found:** A specific Wire Market outlet in the Third Reach operating under the designer's original distribution guidelines — sold only to people who have spent at least an hour hearing the seller explain the Second Voice effect and who then specifically state they understand and want it anyway. The waiting list is long.

### Ashfire Knuckles
*Cybernetic Enhancement | Hand System (Both Hands) | Very Rare | Cursed*

*"The pain is real. The power is real. Everyone who has these makes the same decision about which fact to focus on."*

A full hand replacement — both hands in standard configuration — in a resonant alloy laced with Ashlands Engine Scar contamination. The alloy glows dull orange at the joints and along the metacarpal ridges under physical stress. Under active use, they glow brighter. In full activation, they cast light as a torch.

The hands are hot. Not pleasantly warm — hot. Bearers describe them at rest as feeling like holding their hands over a fire. Under activation, they burn. The bearer feels this. The enhancement does not filter pain. This is not a design flaw.

**Stat Bonus:** +2 IRON
**Card Modification:** All Melee Attack cards using these hands deal +3 damage and add Burn 1 to the target.
**Card Modification — Sustained Burn:** Once per combat as a free action, the bearer activates full heat output for 1 minute. During this time, all Melee Attack cards deal an additional 9 fire damage, the bearer can melt through resonant metal restraints as a free action, and any creature that grapples the bearer takes 5 fire damage per round.
**Card Modification — Engine Scar Strike:** Once per long rest, the bearer channels Ashlands contamination into a single Melee Attack card. On a hit, the target takes an additional 40 fire damage and must succeed on a FRAME check (difficulty 5) or have one cybernetic enhancement malfunction for 1 minute (attacker's choice, if known; malfunctioning enhancements provide none of their card modifications).
**Card Modification:** The bearer is immune to fire damage.
**Side Effect — The Pain:** The enhancement does not suppress heat sensation. At rest: persistent discomfort (DM should acknowledge in description). During Sustained Burn: 4 fire damage per round to the bearer. After Engine Scar Strike: 9 fire damage and one level of exhaustion to the bearer.
**Side Effect — Long-Term Contamination:** After 3 months of integration, the bearer must make a FRAME check (difficulty 4) once per week. Failure means the Ashlands contamination is spreading — the bearer gains one point of Engine Scar corruption (tracked separately). At 5 corruption points: the bearer's eyes glow faintly orange (cannot be concealed). At 10 points: all RESONANCE-based Defense cards take -2. At 15 points: the bearer's biological body begins converting to Ashlands-altered tissue. At this stage, DM and player should discuss what this means for the character.
**Integration Cost:** Hand replacement with Ashlands-alloy integration. Requires a specialist familiar with Engine Scar material — standard technicians either refuse or cannot properly calibrate the contamination threshold. SIGNAL check difficulty 5 with appropriate specialty; a standard technician attempting this is difficulty 7. Recovery: 7 days, during which the bearer runs a fever. Not available through any legitimate channel.

**Where Found:** Ashlands black market, or through a specific Ashlands salvage crew that handles Engine Scar material by necessity and has learned to work with it.

### The Silence Installation
*Cybernetic Enhancement | Neurological Suppressor | Uncommon | Cursed*

*"It turns off the noise. All of it. The problem is that some of the noise was information you needed."*

A single subcutaneous node installed at the base of the skull, approximately the size of a thumbnail. No visible external markers. No glow, no warmth. Nothing. Specifically designed to be undetectable.

Function: suppression. Creates a dampening field around the bearer's Echomind. Resonant signals that would normally be perceived — other people's enhancements, Wire Craft traffic, spiritual residue on objects, the ambient noise of a city full of resonant metal — are filtered. Dramatically, completely filtered.

For people with sensory processing that cannot handle the constant resonant noise of modern Tesshari, the Silence Installation is transformative. For the first time in years, they can be in a city without pain.

The problem takes time to notice.

**Stat Bonus:** None
**Card Modification:** The bearer is immune to RESONANCE-type damage from Wire Craft or signal-source effects. All incoming RESONANCE damage from resonant-metal sources is reduced to 0.
**Card Modification:** The bearer cannot be tracked by resonant signal. Their Echomind is dampened enough to be effectively invisible to signal-detection methods.
**Card Modification:** All RESONANCE-based Perception cards gain +2 in environments with heavy resonant interference (the dampening clears the noise so they can see clearly).
**Side Effect — What The Silence Removes:** The bearer cannot sense danger from resonant-metal sources. All Perception cards used to detect threats that produce resonant signatures take -2. The bearer cannot integrate with resonant-metal items. Previously integrated items lose their integration. The bearer cannot benefit from Iron Sutra resonant spiritual practices.
**Side Effect — Cumulative Suppression:** Each month of integration, the bearer's Echomind suppression deepens by one grade (tracked in 5 grades). At Grade 3, the bearer can no longer feel their own cybernetic enhancements as part of their body — proprioception is disrupted, and enhancements operate but feel foreign. At Grade 5, the bearer's Echomind is fully suppressed — they cannot Ascend, cannot be read by Iron Sutra Readings, cannot communicate with Iron Afterlife entities, and are functionally spiritually inert. Iron Sutra priests describe looking at a Grade 5 Silence bearer as looking at a room with no one in it. Removal reverses the grades, one per month, but reversal is not guaranteed — a FRAME check (difficulty 4) per month determines whether it proceeds normally or stalls. A bearer who reaches Grade 5 and removes the installation may find their Echomind does not return. The Iron Sutra has documented three such cases. All three individuals are still alive. They describe the permanent silence as peaceful, and cannot explain why that is terrifying to the Iron Sutra scholars who study them.
**Integration Cost:** Base-of-skull node. SIGNAL check difficulty 3 — easy, intentionally. Recovery: 1 day. Inexpensive, widely available, no restrictions required. The Wire Market has a version in their standard catalog. The Iron Sutra objects to its sale but lacks the political authority to restrict it. 300 RM.

**Where Found:** Available through salvage-grade installation services throughout all seven Reaches. No restrictions. No prescription required. Widely available.

### The Hunger Wire
*Cybernetic Enhancement | Resonant Drain System | Rare | Cursed*

*"You will take more than you mean to. You will always take more than you mean to. This is not a side effect. This is the function."*

A wire-silk filament array threaded through the bearer's primary contact surfaces — palms, fingertips, soles of the feet. Subcutaneous, invisible, detectable only under resonant scanning. The array integrates with the bearer's Echomind and creates a continuous, low-level drain function: the bearer's body slowly harvests ambient resonant energy from everything they are in sustained contact with.

From environments: a room the bearer sleeps in for a week becomes slightly duller, slightly quieter, the resonant ambient dropping imperceptibly.

From objects: items the bearer handles extensively lose some of their resonant properties. The first sign is usually a resonant item failing to integrate after weeks of the bearer handling it — the integration isn't catching because the item has been partially drained.

From people: the most significant and most problematic function.

**Stat Bonus:** +2 FRAME
**Card Modification:** The bearer restores 3 HP at the start of each of their turns (ambient drain from the environment passively sustaining them).
**Card Modification — Contact Drain:** A creature the bearer is in sustained physical contact with (grapple, extended handshake, sleeping next to for a full rest) loses 2 HP maximum per contact period; the bearer gains the same as temporary HP. The creature is not aware this is happening.
**Card Modification — Deep Draw:** Once per long rest, the bearer may fully activate the drain through deliberate touch. The target must succeed on a FRAME check (difficulty 5). On a failure, the bearer drains 11 HP maximum from the target and gains the same as regular HP. On a success, the bearer drains only 3 HP maximum. The target does not know what happened — they simply feel vaguely tired.
**Side Effect — The Hunger:** After one month of integration: the healing feels insufficient. The bearer becomes aware, in a persistent low-level way, that they want more. After two months: the bearer must succeed on a RESONANCE check (difficulty 4) each week or use the Contact Drain on someone they are close to (ally, loved one, trusted companion) without intending to — casual touch, lingering contact, without fully acknowledging why. After four months: difficulty increases to 5. The bearer has difficulty spending time around people without feeling the drain opportunity. They begin to find reasons to touch people. After six months: Contact Drain is always active, automatically triggering on all extended physical contact whether the bearer wants it to or not.
**Integration Cost:** Multi-site subcutaneous installation. SIGNAL check difficulty 4, 4 hours. Typically performed without full disclosure of the long-term drain function — sold as "ambient resonance enhancement," which is technically accurate and practically misleading. 2,000 RM through deceptive channels.

**Where Found:** Sold under deceptive names by a Wire Market black-market affiliate in the Second Reach. The Wire Market officially does not produce or endorse it. The affiliate officially does not exist. Both of these statements are technically true in the ways that matter for liability.

The DM should consider not just the mechanical value but the history each piece carries — who made it, who used it before, what the metal has absorbed. In Tesshari, nothing you put in your body is simply a tool.*
