*Notable pieces — not standard modifications, but items with histories and particular properties.*

---

> "There is no such thing as a neutral enhancement. Every piece of metal you bond to your body was made by someone with an opinion, used by someone with a history, and carries whatever resonance those things left behind. Standard modifications are built to minimize this. The interesting ones are not."
>

## A Note on Enhancement Installation

Standard cybernetic enhancements require a skilled installer (a Wire Craft technician or equivalent), downtime proportional to the enhancement's complexity, and a recovery period. The enhancements listed here are non-standard — unique, historically significant, or sufficiently unusual that installation carries additional considerations.

Unless otherwise noted:
- **Installation Time:** 8 hours (surgical, requiring a DC 15 Medicine check by the installer; failure means the installation requires 16 hours and the recipient suffers 2 levels of exhaustion)
- **Recovery:** 3 days of light activity only
- **Removal:** Possible but costly (same installation time, recipient takes 2d8 damage from cellular disruption)
- **Resonant Bonding:** Most notable enhancements require 1 week of bonding before their unique properties activate

# Widow's Architecture
*Very Rare Combat Enhancement — Integrated Blade System | Requires Attunement*

## Description

A full forearm replacement — from elbow to fingertip — in a dark resonant alloy that catches light differently depending on the angle, giving the arm a quality of depth rather than surface. At rest, it appears as a high-quality limb replacement. The integration point at the elbow is seamless; the cellular bonding is exceptional work.

When activated, the forearm reconfigures: the hand folds back, the dorsal surface of the forearm opens along three seams, and three resonant blades extend — not uniformly, but at the specific angles calculated for maximum efficiency in close quarters. The blades are fixed-angle, not articulated. They are always at exactly the right angle. This is by design: the calculation is built in.

The system was named "Widow's Architecture" by the second person who used it, after the builder's wife, who was killed in the Second Reach's infrastructure wars. The original builder is dead. The name stayed.

## Installation Requirements

Full forearm amputation (or replacement of an existing prosthetic). The integration requires a Wire Craft technician with specialty in structural-combat systems (DC 18 Medicine check; a standard technician can attempt at DC 22). The cellular bonding process is intensive: 5 days recovery, no exceptions.

## Properties

- The integrated blades function as three simultaneous resonant short blades. When the bearer makes an attack with the system, they may attack twice instead of once (this replaces, not adds to, the Extra Attack feature if the bearer has it).
- **Combat Geometry.** The system's angle calculation extends to the bearer's full combat awareness. The bearer has advantage on attack rolls made against creatures within 5 feet, and opportunity attacks made with the system deal an additional 2d6 damage.
- **Retraction Speed.** The system can extend or retract as a free action (not a bonus action). The bearer is never caught in a social situation with visible blades.
- Once per long rest, the bearer may activate a full extension — all three blades at maximum extension, angled for a single devastating strike. This attack deals 6d8 piercing damage, ignores half cover and three-quarters cover, and forces the target to make a DC 17 Constitution saving throw or be incapacitated until the end of their next turn.

**Attunement Note:** The system attunes to the bearer's nervous system automatically within 1 week of installation. A bearer who removes it (if it can be removed — the integration makes this nearly impossible without the above procedure) loses the attunement immediately. The system cannot be attuned by anyone who has never been in genuine mortal danger. The architecture recognizes readiness.

## History and Lore

Built by a weapons artificer named Toro-En in the early years of the Second Reach's internal wars. Toro-En was known for her extreme precision — her systems were notoriously difficult to install but never malfunctioned. She built three of this model. The first was destroyed in combat. The second is this one. The third was lost in the Ashlands during an Ashlands incursion twenty years ago and has not been recovered.

Toro-En herself had both arms replaced with her own designs. She died at the age of seventy-three of cardiac failure — nothing dramatic. Her systems outlasted her.

## Where Found

Held by a retired soldier in the Fourth Reach who has been trying to sell it for three years and keeps refusing every offer, not because the offers are inadequate but because she cannot fully explain why she doesn't want to let it go. She was the one who named it. She killed her wife's killer with it. The two things are connected in her.

# The Convergence Engine
*Rare Combat Enhancement — Integrated Pulse Arm | Requires Attunement*

## Description

A shoulder-and-upper-arm replacement that integrates a pulse-arm discharge system into the deltoid structure. The system is compact enough to be concealed under standard clothing with loose outer garments. The discharge aperture is on the palm — a wire-mesh ring with a hollow center that channels the pulse discharge through the natural axis of an outstretched arm.

The system has an audible charge cycle — a high frequency that builds for two seconds before discharge. Anyone who has heard it once recognizes it immediately. This is a feature, not a flaw: experienced users of this model create tactical situations where the sound does work before the shot does.

## Installation Requirements

Shoulder and upper arm replacement. Requires access to a power source for the capacitor array — the system has a biological-power interface (draws from the bearer's metabolic process) that recharges the capacitor over 6 hours of normal activity. Installation requires DC 16 Medicine check; failure causes the capacitor array to bond improperly and must be reinstalled (adding 3 days and one additional level of exhaustion).

## Properties

- The pulse arm deals 3d8 force damage at range (30/90 feet), targeting one creature. No spell slot required. Recharges after 2 rounds of biological recharge (automatic).
- **Overcharge.** Once per short rest, the bearer may overcharge the capacitor — drawing additional metabolic energy — to fire a concentrated burst that deals 6d8 force damage in a 15-foot cone. All creatures in the cone make a DC 15 Strength saving throw or are pushed back 10 feet and knocked prone. After overcharge, the bearer suffers one level of exhaustion.
- **Resonant Alignment.** Pulse discharges from this system deal double damage to creatures that are heavily cybernetically enhanced (more than 50% of the body replaced). The system was designed for anti-armor combat and remembers it.
- The bearer has resistance to force damage.

**Drawback.** The metabolic drain is constant. The bearer must consume twice the normal amount of food and water to prevent exhaustion buildup. In field conditions without reliable supplies, this is significant.

## History and Lore

The Convergence Engine was a mass-produced model during the Second Reach's militia period — there were several hundred of them. This one is notable because it was used by a militia commander named Seta-Orai during the Twelve-Day Siege of the Crescent Gate, where she fired more than two hundred overcharge discharges in twelve days and was in continuous combat for the final seventy-two hours. At the end of the siege, she collapsed and could not be woken for four days.

She woke up and immediately asked if the gate had held. It had. She is still alive. She is seventy-one years old and runs a small foundry. She still has the system. She sold it to pay a debt, then bought it back six months later without explaining why.

This particular unit has her metabolic signature still faintly embedded in the capacitor calibration. A bearer who attunes to it occasionally feels, in moments of extremity, a bone-deep tiredness that is not their own — and a bone-deep determination that is not their own either.

## Where Found

Currently for sale at a Wire Market outlet in the Second Reach that specializes in military-grade used equipment. Priced high but priced correctly. The listing notes the metabolic requirements. It does not note the other things.

# Hollow Strike Array
*Very Rare Combat Enhancement — Full Leg System | Requires Attunement*

## Description

A full leg replacement from the hip — both legs, in the standard configuration, though single-leg versions exist and are significantly less expensive. The legs are longer than biological standard by approximately four centimeters — not enough to look aberrant, enough to matter in movement. The musculature is wire-silk over resonant-metal bone structure, with a strike-surface embedded in the heel and ball of each foot.

At rest, the legs look like high-quality cybernetic replacements. Under movement: they are quiet in a way that biological legs are not. No impact sound. The strike surfaces absorb the sound of footfall completely. Walking, a user of the Hollow Strike Array is essentially silent.

The strike surfaces function as weapons — each heel strike deals 2d8 bludgeoning damage when activated. The activation is a mental impulse, imperceptible from outside.

## Installation Requirements

Hip socket modification and dual leg replacement. This is major surgery: DC 18 Medicine check, 8-hour procedure, 7-day recovery minimum. Should only be performed by a Wire Craft technician with structural-systems specialty. The hip socket modification is permanent; even if the legs are later replaced, the socket modification remains.

## Properties

- The bearer moves silently. Not with advantage on Stealth checks — silently. No sound of movement is produced by the legs. This does not affect the rest of the bearer's body, so armor, equipment, and other noise sources still apply, but footfall specifically: nothing.
- **Ground Strike.** The bearer may use a bonus action to activate the strike surface on one or both feet. On the next move action, any creature the bearer passes through (within 5 feet) makes a DC 16 Dexterity saving throw or takes 2d8 bludgeoning damage and is knocked prone. This works on enemies who would not normally be vulnerable to being knocked prone by movement.
- **Kinetic Conversion.** When the bearer falls, they may choose to convert all fall damage to force damage discharged at a single creature within 10 feet of their landing point. The discharge damage equals the fall damage that would have been taken; the bearer takes none.
- The bearer's movement speed increases by 15 feet.

**Attunement Note:** The legs attune during the recovery period — bone-to-metal interface has 7 days to calibrate. A bearer who is not sufficiently rested during the calibration period (fails to complete long rests) must extend the calibration by 1 day per failed rest. The legs are patient about this. They wait.

## History and Lore

Designed by a Wire Market research team for a specific operational purpose — deep infiltration, kinetic-entry breach work. The full production run was twelve units. Seven were deployed to field operatives. Two were destroyed in operations. Two were recovered from operatives who died in the field. One was never deployed. This is the one that was never deployed, still in its original carry case with the Wire Market's technical documentation.

The Wire Market released it into civilian sale eighteen months ago when the program was discontinued. The program was discontinued because three of the seven deployed operatives went missing within six months of deployment — not killed, not captured, simply not heard from. The program lead filed a report that used the phrase "self-directed operational divergence," which is bureaucratic language for "they stopped doing what we sent them to do." The legs make the bearer very good at being somewhere quietly, alone, without being followed. Apparently this turns out to have other uses.

## Where Found

Wire Market civilian sale, First Reach outlet. Listed as a surplus item. The documentation in the case is complete. The Wire Market has not included in the listing the information about the three missing operatives. If someone read the original program files, they would find that all three missing operatives left their last known location in the direction of the Ashlands.

# The Patient Eye
*Rare Utility Enhancement — Optical System | No Attunement Required*

## Description

An eye replacement — single eye, the right side in all known installations, though left-side versions can theoretically be produced. The iris is a fixed, pale grey — not the standard dark resonant-metal look of most optical replacements, but a particular grey that people who meet the bearer find slightly unnerving before they understand why: the eye does not blink at normal frequency. It blinks when necessary. It is assessing.

The optical system includes standard visual enhancements (low-light, magnification) but its distinctive function is in the temporal dimension: the system has a predictive analysis layer that calculates probable positions, trajectories, and actions based on observed motion patterns.

It does not give perfect foresight. It gives very good guesses, at high speed, from a great deal of data.

## Properties

- Low-light vision to 120 feet; darkvision to 60 feet.
- Magnification up to 20x.
- **Pattern Recognition.** After observing a creature for one full round, the bearer has advantage on attack rolls against that creature for the remainder of the encounter. The eye is tracking movement patterns.
- **Intercept Calculation.** Once per short rest, when the bearer makes a ranged attack, they may activate an intercept calculation. The attack automatically hits (no roll required), but cannot result in a critical hit. This function represents pure trajectory calculation: it is guaranteed but unspectacular.
- The bearer cannot be surprised if their eye is unobstructed.

## History and Lore

Originally developed as an intelligence tool — designed for surveillance analysts who needed to read behavioral patterns in crowds. The function transferred neatly to combat applications. The design is old — forty years — and the production run was large. This one is notable because its optical record contains approximately 8,000 hours of surveillance footage that the previous user never wiped. The images are locked in a read-only archive state. A Wire Craft specialist with the right tools could access them. It is unclear what is in them. The previous user sold the eye quickly and cheaply and has not been findable since.

## Where Found

Black market auction circuit, Third Reach. Priced below market because of the data concern. The listing says "optical archive present, unreviewed." This is accurate. A buyer who knows what they are looking for would know that's either worthless or very valuable, and would probably buy it.

# Threadwalker Interface
*Very Rare Utility Enhancement — Neural Spine System | Requires Attunement*

## Description

A spinal column augmentation — not a replacement but an addition, installed along the posterior cervical spine as a series of resonant metal nodes connected by wire-silk conduit. The installation is visible as a slight ridge along the back of the neck, typically concealed under clothing. Under the right resonant-light conditions, the conduit glows faintly gold.

The system interfaces the bearer's Echomind directly with the Wire Craft network — not through external devices but through direct neural signal. The bearer can sense the Wire network's presence, access public Wire Craft channels without hardware, and with concentration, read the surface-level resonant signals of digital communications in their environment.

## Installation Requirements

Cervical spinal modification. This procedure has a significant risk profile: DC 20 Medicine check, with failure meaning the installer must roll again or cause permanent Dexterity damage (1d4 to Dexterity score, minimum 8). Requires an installer with Wire Craft specialty, not just surgical skill — the neural interface must be calibrated to the specific Echomind signature of the recipient.

## Properties

**While Attuned:**

- The bearer can sense the presence of active Wire Craft signals within 300 feet. They do not read the content, but they know there is traffic, the direction of the source, and rough volume/intensity.
- **Network Presence.** The bearer can access public Wire Craft channels without hardware, as though carrying a standard signal device. They can transmit and receive text-equivalent communications silently.
- **Signal Read.** Once per short rest, the bearer may concentrate for one minute on a specific resonant signal source within 60 feet. They read the surface content of the most recent transmission — not encrypted data, but the equivalent of an intercepted spoken message. This requires concentration (as a spell).
- **Deep Interface.** Once per long rest, the bearer may enter full network immersion for up to 10 minutes. During this time, they are unconscious in the physical world but fully present in the Wire Craft network, able to move through it, access unsecured nodes, and observe network traffic. Their physical body is vulnerable during this time.

**Drawback.** The neural interface means the bearer is always slightly in the network. They have disadvantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks in environments with heavy Wire Craft traffic. Cities are difficult. Wire Market facilities are nearly overwhelming.

## History and Lore

The Threadwalker Interface was a Wire Market experimental project — six units produced, three of which killed their recipients during installation (before the procedure protocol was corrected). Two of the surviving three were Wire Market operatives. This unit belonged to an operative named Keth-Vaon who disappeared into the Wire Craft deep-network fourteen months ago and has not emerged. When Wire Market technicians reviewed the network logs, they found she was still in there — not stuck, but moving. Choosing to stay. Her physical body died after thirty-six hours without care. Her signal is still present in the deep network, navigating, going somewhere.

This unit was removed from her body after her death. It was cleaned, calibrated, and placed back in circulation. The Wire Market's ethics board raised concerns. The ethics board was overruled.

## Where Found

Wire Market direct sale, high clearance required. The Wire Market considers this unit fully functional and appropriately priced. They do not mention Keth-Vaon in the product documentation. A thorough search of Wire Market records by a skilled investigator would find her case file. It is marked "Operational Outcome: Anomalous." It has not been closed.

# Cartographer's Sense
*Uncommon Utility Enhancement — Proprioceptive System | No Attunement Required*

## Description

A distributed system: small resonant nodes installed in the palms of both hands, both feet, and the base of the skull. The nodes are subcutaneous — invisible, detectable only as slight warmth to a careful touch. The system integrates with the bearer's proprioceptive nervous system, giving them a constant awareness of their exact position in three-dimensional space.

There is no dramatic visual component. This is an internal enhancement. People who have it often describe feeling, for the first time, exactly where they are.

## Properties

- The bearer always knows their exact cardinal direction and altitude above sea level.
- The bearer has advantage on Athletics checks involving balance, climbing, and spatial movement.
- The bearer cannot become lost by mundane means. They may not know the direction to a destination, but they know exactly where they are.
- After spending 1 hour in a new environment (building, district, natural terrain feature), the bearer can reproduce a precise map of it from memory, accurate to within 2 feet.
- The bearer has advantage on saving throws against being knocked prone.

**Note.** This system has no historic significance or unusual resonance. It is simply excellent work, produced by a salvage artificer in the Third Reach who was tired of watching people get lost in the Ashlands approaches. The artificer sells them at a fair price and refuses to be notable about it.

## Where Found

Available from the artificer Nen-Dao, Third Reach, Holding 7. He has a waiting list. He is a slow and careful worker. He will not rush. Anyone who asks him to rush is removed from the waiting list. He has told his clients this and they have accepted it because his work is worth the wait.

# The Between-Sense
*Very Rare Spiritual Enhancement — Echomind Resonator | Requires Attunement*

## Description

An installation at the base of the skull and both temples — three nodes of a rare resonant alloy that is described in Iron Sutra texts as "the metal that remembers the threshold." The nodes are visible as small raised points under the skin, dark against flesh, warm to external touch. They pulse, very faintly, in a rhythm that does not match the bearer's heartbeat.

The sensation of wearing the Between-Sense is described by users as feeling like standing in a doorway — always. Not unpleasantly. Just... present at a threshold. The sense of being between two places at once.

## Installation Requirements

Requires an Iron Sutra-trained installation practitioner — this is not a Wire Craft installation, it is a spiritual procedure as much as a physical one. The installing priest must be able to calibrate the nodes to the bearer's specific Echomind signature, which requires a Spiritual Reading (see Services) and 4 hours of ritual calibration before the physical installation. Installation itself takes 2 hours. Recovery is 3 days.

## Properties

**While Attuned:**

- The bearer can perceive the presence of the Iron Afterlife's near-plane — the layer closest to the physical world where recently-dead Echominds gather before moving deeper. They sense this as a kind of pressure or presence, not visual. They cannot communicate through this perception without additional tools, but they know when the dead are nearby.
- **Between-Speaking.** The bearer may attempt to communicate with an Echomind that has been dead for less than 1 year (it has not yet moved too deep). This requires a DC 15 Wisdom check and one minute of focus. On a success, a brief exchange (equivalent to the *Speak with Dead* spell, but limited to recently-dead Echominds rather than physical remains) is possible. On a failure, the between-layer responds with static — a disorienting flood of partial signals from many Echominds that causes 2d6 psychic damage.
- The bearer has advantage on Wisdom saving throws against effects originating from the Iron Afterlife or from creatures that are partially present in the between-plane.
- **Iron Sutra Resonance.** When an Iron Sutra priest performs a Reading on the bearer, the Reading is more detailed than usual. The priest may ask one additional question.

**Drawback.** The between-sense does not filter. The bearer is always perceiving the near-plane. In areas where people have recently died in large numbers — battlefields, hospital districts, Ashlands border zones — the pressure is overwhelming. The bearer has disadvantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks in these areas as the near-plane noise drowns out the physical world.

## History and Lore

Developed by the Iron Sutra's second-generation scholars as a tool for spiritual research. Forty were ever made. Most are accounted for — held by active Iron Sutra practitioners who use them in their work. This particular unit was installed in a child — not the bearer's choice, a decision made by parents who wanted their child to be a priest. The child grew up, became a priest, left the order at thirty-five, and sold the enhancement. They described the experience of always being in the doorway as something that made them very good at their work and very bad at their life. They are now a salvage trader in the Sixth Reach and reports that they still miss it, occasionally, in the way you miss a door you used to know the way through.

## Where Found

Iron Sutra installation practitioners can source them with sufficient lead time. The Iron Sutra does not freely sell them to non-members — a bearer who is not Iron Sutra affiliated will need either a significant donation, a personal connection, or a compelling reason. They are not illegal to own. They are considered sacred technology by the Iron Sutra and treated accordingly.

# Afterlife Anchor
*Rare Spiritual Enhancement — Resonant Heart Casing | Requires Attunement*

## Description

A resonant metal casing installed around the biological heart — not replacing it, reinforcing it. The procedure coats the heart in a resonant mesh that bonds at the cellular level. From the outside: a slight luminescence visible through thin skin over the sternum, in the specific register of pale resonant light. The bearer's heartbeat, when listened to through resonant-attuned equipment, produces a secondary signal in a register that should not be physically possible from a biological heart.

The casing creates a literal anchor for the bearer's Echomind. The soul is not prevented from leaving the body. But leaving is harder.

## Installation Requirements

Open-chest procedure. The single most dangerous installation in regular use — failure rate approximately 12% even with a skilled Wire Craft surgeon. DC 20 Medicine check; failure results in complication (1d6: 1-2 bearer takes 4d10 piercing damage and requires stabilization; 3-4 procedure fails, must be re-attempted; 5-6 heart is damaged and bearer has -2 Constitution until magical or Wire Craft healing is applied). Recovery: 14 days, bed rest for the first 5.

## Properties

**While Attuned:**

- The bearer has resistance to necrotic damage.
- **Death Resistance.** When the bearer is reduced to 0 hit points for the first time in an encounter, they may make a DC 15 Constitution saving throw. On a success, they are reduced to 1 hit point instead. The Anchor holds. This can only occur once per encounter.
- **Echomind Stability.** The bearer cannot be possessed, cannot have their soul displaced, and cannot be subject to any effect that removes their Echomind from their body involuntarily. The casing is literal: the soul is anchored.
- **Signal Strength.** Iron Afterlife effects that target the bearer require the caster/source to succeed on a DC 16 ability check (Wisdom for spiritual entities, Intelligence for technological sources) or fail entirely. The Anchor is a strong signal and resists interference.

**Drawback.** The casing also makes the Iron Afterlife harder for the bearer to perceive. They have disadvantage on any check or saving throw related to perceiving, communicating with, or understanding Iron Afterlife phenomena. The anchor works in both directions.

## History and Lore

The Afterlife Anchor became significant during the Third Convergence War, when Iron Afterlife incursion tactics became standard military doctrine. Units exposed to Iron Afterlife weaponry suffered complete Echomind displacement at catastrophic rates. The Anchor was a field-developed solution — improvised by a Wire Craft field surgeon named Oru-Tesshai who bolted a resonant mesh onto dying soldiers' hearts with whatever was available because the alternative was watching their souls get removed while their bodies kept fighting.

Oru-Tesshai's field solution worked. She died in the same battle, before she could be properly credited. The refined version of her technique has saved several thousand lives since. Her name is in a footnote in a military engineering manual. The Iron Sutra holds a small annual observance in her honor that they have not told the military about.

## Where Found

Available through Wire Craft surgical facilities, uncommon but not rare. The price is substantial and the risk is substantial. People who need it know where to find it. People who are considering it are advised to consider the failure rate first.

# The Listening Wire
*Very Rare Spiritual Enhancement — Echomind Amplification Array | Requires Attunement*

## Description

A series of wire-silk filaments installed through the cervical spine, terminating in sensor nodes distributed across the skull. The installation is subcutaneous and largely invisible; close inspection of the back of the neck reveals faint seams. The effect is felt rather than seen: the bearer's Echomind resonance broadens in range and sensitivity, able to perceive the spiritual weight of objects, places, and entities that have resonant history.

The sensation is described as similar to suddenly being able to hear in a room where you were previously deaf — not a new sense added on top of existing ones, but the realization that the existing sense was operating at a fraction of its capacity.

## Installation Requirements

Cervical and cranial installation. Requires an Iron Sutra practitioner with both surgical and spiritual training — the filament termination points must be precisely mapped to the bearer's Echomind architecture, which is unique. Installing without this mapping has resulted in bearers whose Echomind resonance became too broad to filter, leaving them in a state of permanent, unmanageable spiritual overwhelm. DC 17 Medicine and DC 15 Religion check required (both from the installer, or two different installers). Recovery: 5 days.

## Properties

**While Attuned:**

- The bearer can sense the resonant history of any object they touch — how long it has existed, how many hands have held it, the intensity of events that occurred around it. This is sensation, not language: strong impressions, emotions, the shape of events without their details. A Wisdom check (DC 12 for general sense, DC 17 for specific information) translates sensation into useful knowledge.
- The bearer can sense when a nearby creature (within 60 feet) is hiding strong emotion. Not what the emotion is — that it exists and that it is strong.
- **Spirit Resonance.** When the bearer interacts with an Iron Afterlife entity or a creature with significant spiritual power, they have advantage on all Charisma checks made in that interaction. The entity recognizes the Listening Wire and treats the bearer as spiritually literate.
- Once per long rest, the bearer may attune to a location — pressing both palms to the ground or walls and concentrating for one minute. They receive the emotional history of the most significant events that occurred in that location, going back up to 100 years. The DM provides this as sensory impression.

**Drawback.** The bearer cannot touch objects without receiving some impression of their history. Objects with violent or sorrowful histories are difficult. The bearer has disadvantage on any task requiring fine manipulation in environments where they have not had time to acclimate (approximately 10 minutes of desensitization required in new locations).

## Where Found

Available only through specialized Iron Sutra installation practitioners. Not purchasable through standard Wire Market channels — the spiritual mapping component requires Iron Sutra knowledge. A non-Iron Sutra applicant would need either a relationship with an Iron Sutra priest of sufficient rank or access to the black market spiritual-enhancement circuit that exists in the shadow of the Iron Sutra's official monopoly.

# The Second Voice
*Rare Cursed Enhancement — Vocal System | Requires Attunement*

## "The voice it gives you is worth everything. The voice it also gives you is not yours, and it will say things in your sleep."

## Description

A throat and vocal apparatus enhancement — a resonant metal insert that integrates with the larynx and resonance chambers, augmenting the bearer's voice with additional harmonic registers. The enhancement makes the bearer's voice extraordinary: richer, clearer, capable of frequencies that standard biological voices cannot produce. It is, simply, the most beautiful voice the bearer has ever had.

The installation leaves a slight visible seam along the neck — careful tailoring covers it. The voice is immediately noticeable. People look toward the bearer when they speak. They listen without meaning to.

The problem is the second voice.

## Installation Requirements

Throat installation: DC 14 Medicine check, 4 hours, 3-day recovery. Significantly easier than most notable enhancements. This is part of why people keep installing it despite the known issues.

## Properties

**While Attuned:**

- The bearer's voice becomes extraordinarily compelling. They have advantage on all Charisma checks that involve speaking and a +4 bonus to Charisma (Performance) checks.
- The bearer can produce tones that affect the emotional state of listeners. Once per short rest, all creatures within 30 feet that can hear the bearer make a DC 14 Wisdom saving throw or have their emotional state shifted as the bearer intends (frightened, calmed, motivated, despaired). This is not a mind-control effect — it shifts the emotional baseline. Creatures can act against this shifted state, but it takes effort.
- The bearer can project their voice to a range of 300 feet with perfect clarity and at any volume from barely audible to structurally disruptive (5d6 thunder damage in a 10-foot cone, creatures within 30 feet make a DC 16 Constitution saving throw or are deafened for 1 hour, usable once per long rest).

**The Second Voice.** During sleep, the bearer speaks. Not meaninglessly — the second voice uses the enhanced vocal range to produce speech, and the speech is informative. This is the curse:

- What the second voice says is the bearer's deepest, most unguarded thoughts — the things the bearer would not say consciously.
- Anyone within hearing range during a long rest hears this speech.
- The bearer does not wake and does not remember what was said.
- The speech covers topics the bearer is actively avoiding. Whatever they are suppressing, the Second Voice finds it.

Long-term bearers of the enhancement develop strategies: sleeping alone, sleeping with noise to mask it, exhausting themselves so completely that sleep is too short for the Voice to work. None of these strategies are reliable.

**Why People Use It Anyway.** The voice it gives is real. The Second Voice is the cost, and the cost has a quality of honesty to it that long-term bearers sometimes describe, reluctantly, as therapeutic. They find out what they are actually feeling. They just find out in public.

## History and Lore

Designed by a Wire Craft artist — someone who considered their work aesthetic as much as functional — who was interested in the relationship between voice and authenticity. The Second Voice effect was documented in early testing. The designer released the enhancement anyway, considering it a feature: "The voice you want and the voice you have. Both of them yours." They were, and remain, controversial.

There are approximately sixty of these in circulation. Bearers tend to know each other, in a loose network that shares sleeping strategies and processes the social damage from public revelation of private feelings. It is a small community and a strange one but genuine.

## Where Found

Available through a specific Wire Market outlet in the Third Reach that operates under the designer's original distribution guidelines — the enhancement is sold only to people who have spent at least an hour hearing the seller explain the Second Voice effect and who then specifically state that they understand and want it anyway. The waiting list is long.

# Ashfire Knuckles
*Very Rare Cursed Combat Enhancement — Hand System | Requires Attunement*

## "The pain is real. The power is real. Everyone who has these makes the same decision about which fact to focus on."

## Description

A full hand replacement — both hands, in the standard configuration — in a resonant alloy that has been laced with Ashlands Engine Scar contamination. The alloy glows dull orange at the joints and along the metacarpal ridges when the bearer is under physical stress. Under active use, they glow brighter. In full activation, they cast light as a torch, the orange light of something burning without fuel.

The hands are warm. Not pleasantly warm — hot. Bearers describe them as feeling, at rest, like holding their hands over a fire. Under activation, they burn. The bearer feels this. The enhancement does not filter pain. This is not a design flaw.

## Installation Requirements

Hand replacement with Ashlands-alloy integration. Requires a specialist — not a standard Wire Craft technician, but someone familiar with Engine Scar material. Standard technicians either refuse or cannot properly calibrate the contamination threshold. Incorrect calibration results in the contamination spreading to the installation site. DC 19 Medicine check with the appropriate specialty; a standard technician attempting this is DC 25. Recovery: 7 days, during which the bearer runs a fever.

## Properties

**While Attuned:**

- Unarmed strikes with the Ashfire Knuckles deal 2d8 bludgeoning + 1d8 fire damage.
- **Sustained Burn.** As a bonus action, the bearer activates full heat output. For the next minute, their strikes deal an additional 2d8 fire damage, they can melt through resonant metal restraints as an action, and any creature that grapples them takes 1d8 fire damage per round.
- **Engine Scar Strike.** Once per long rest, the bearer may channel the Ashlands contamination into a single strike. On a hit, the target takes 8d10 fire damage and must succeed on a DC 17 Constitution saving throw or have their cybernetic enhancements malfunction for 1 minute (enhancements provide no bonuses, and any active systems shut down). Engine Scar contamination at concentrated levels disrupts resonant metal function.
- The bearer is immune to fire damage.

**The Pain.** The enhancement does not suppress the heat sensation. The bearer feels everything:
- At rest: persistent discomfort (not mechanical, but the DM should acknowledge it in description)
- Under Sustained Burn: 1d6 fire damage per round, taken by the bearer
- After Engine Scar Strike: 2d10 fire damage and one level of exhaustion

**Long-Term Contamination.** After 3 months of attunement, the bearer must make a DC 15 Constitution saving throw once per week. Failure means the Ashlands contamination is spreading: the bearer gains one point of Engine Scar corruption (tracked separately). At 5 corruption points: the bearer's eyes glow faintly orange (cannot be concealed). At 10 points: the bearer has disadvantage on Wisdom saving throws. At 15 points: the bearer's biological body begins to convert to Ashlands-altered tissue. At this stage, the DM and player should discuss what this means for the character.

**Why People Use Them Anyway.** The Engine Scar Strike is the most effective anti-cybernetic weapon known to exist. In the current period, with the Seventh Convergence approaching and heavily-enhanced forces on every side, this is a weapon that changes outcomes. The pain is real. The contamination is real. The power is real. People make this calculation.

## Where Found

Ashlands black market, or through a specific Ashlands salvage crew that handles Engine Scar material by necessity and has learned to work with it. Not available through any legitimate channel.

# The Silence Installation
*Uncommon Cursed Utility Enhancement — Neurological Suppressor | Requires Attunement*

## "It turns off the noise. All of it. The problem is that some of the noise was information you needed."

## Description

A single subcutaneous node installed at the base of the skull, approximately the size of a thumbnail. No visible external markers. No glowing seams, no warmth. Nothing. The installation is specifically designed to be undetectable.

Its function is suppression: the Silence Installation creates a dampening field around the bearer's Echomind. Resonant signals that would normally be perceived — other people's enhancements, Wire Craft traffic, spiritual residue on objects, the ambient noise of a city full of resonant metal — are filtered. Dramatically, completely filtered.

For people with sensory processing that cannot handle the constant resonant noise of modern Tesshari, the Silence Installation is transformative. For the first time in years, they can be in a city without pain. They can think. They can sleep.

The problem takes time to notice.

## Installation Requirements

Base-of-skull node installation. DC 12 Medicine check — easy, intentionally. Recovery: 1 day. This is the most accessible enhancement on this list. The accessibility is part of the problem.

## Properties

**While Attuned:**

- The bearer is immune to the noise and distraction of resonant metal environments. Cities, Wire Market facilities, the Ashlands approach — all of them become quiet. The bearer can think and function without the constant resonant background.
- The bearer is immune to psychic damage from resonant-metal-source effects (Wire Craft weapons, spiritual resonance attacks).
- The bearer cannot be tracked by resonant signal. Their Echomind is dampened enough to be effectively invisible to signal-detection methods.
- The bearer has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks in environments with heavy resonant interference (the dampening clears the noise so they can see clearly).

**What The Silence Removes:**

- The bearer cannot sense danger from resonant-metal sources. Iron Afterlife entities, Wire Craft weapons charging, cybernetically-enhanced creatures moving in ways that produce resonant signatures — none of this is perceptible. They have disadvantage on Perception checks made to detect threats that produce resonant signatures.
- The bearer's Echomind is suppressed. They cannot attune to resonant-metal items. Any previously attuned items attunement is broken. They cannot benefit from Iron Sutra resonant spiritual practices.
- **Cumulative Suppression.** Each month of attunement, the bearer's Echomind suppression deepens by one grade (tracked in 5 grades). At Grade 3, the bearer can no longer feel their own cybernetic enhancements as part of their body — proprioception is disrupted, and enhancements operate but feel foreign. At Grade 5, the bearer's Echomind is fully suppressed — they cannot Ascend, cannot be read by Iron Sutra Readings, cannot communicate with Iron Afterlife entities, and are functionally spiritually inert. Iron Sutra priests describe looking at a Grade 5 Silence bearer as looking at a room with no one in it.

**Removal reverses the grades, one per month, but the reversal is not guaranteed.** A DC 15 Constitution saving throw per month determines whether the reversal proceeds normally or stalls. A bearer who reaches Grade 5 and then removes the installation may find that their Echomind does not return. The Iron Sutra has documented three such cases. All three individuals are still alive. They describe the permanent silence as peaceful, and cannot explain why that is terrifying to the Iron Sutra scholars who study them.

**Why People Use It Anyway.** The resonant noise of modern Tesshari is genuinely debilitating for a significant portion of the population whose Echomind sensitivity is above baseline. The Silence Installation solves a real problem. The long-term consequences are real but distant. The relief is immediate. The math makes sense in the short term, and the short term is what people are living in.

## Where Found

Available through salvage-grade installation services throughout all seven Reaches. No restrictions. No prescription required. Inexpensive. Widely available. The Wire Market has a version in their standard catalog. The Iron Sutra objects to its sale but lacks the political authority to restrict it.

# The Hunger Wire
*Rare Cursed Spiritual Enhancement — Resonant Drain System | Requires Attunement*

## "You will take more than you mean to. You will always take more than you mean to. This is not a side effect. This is the function."

## Description

A wire-silk filament array threaded through the bearer's primary contact surfaces — the palms, the fingertips, the soles of the feet. Subcutaneous, invisible, detectable only under resonant scanning. The array integrates with the bearer's Echomind and creates a continuous, low-level drain function: the bearer's body slowly harvests ambient resonant energy from everything they are in sustained contact with.

From environments: a room the bearer sleeps in for a week becomes slightly duller, slightly quieter, the resonant ambient dropping imperceptibly.

From objects: items the bearer handles extensively lose some of their resonant properties. The first sign is usually a resonant item failing to attune after weeks of the bearer handling it — the attunement isn't catching because the item has been partially drained.

From people: the most significant and most problematic function. Physical contact with a creature over time transfers resonant energy from that creature to the bearer.

## Installation Requirements

Multi-site subcutaneous installation. DC 14 Medicine check, 4 hours. Not difficult. The difficulty is in knowing what you are installing. The installation is typically performed without full disclosure of the long-term drain function — it is sold as "ambient resonance enhancement," which is technically accurate and practically misleading.

## Properties

**While Attuned:**

- The bearer heals 1d6 hit points per hour of rest, drawn from ambient resonant energy. This requires no action and provides a persistent low-level healing.
- **Contact Drain.** A creature that the bearer is in sustained physical contact with (grapple, extended handshake, sleeping next to for a full rest) loses 1d4 hit point maximum per contact period, and the bearer gains the same as temporary hit points. The creature is not aware this is happening and does not feel it.
- **Deep Draw.** Once per long rest, the bearer may fully activate the drain through deliberate touch. The target makes a DC 16 Constitution saving throw. On a failure, the bearer drains 2d10 hit point maximum from the target and gains the same as regular hit points (not temporary). On a success, the bearer drains only 1d6. The target does not know what happened — they simply feel vaguely tired.
- The bearer's Constitution score increases by 2 while attuned (maximum 22).

**The Hunger.** The enhancement creates dependency. After one month of attunement:
- The healing function feels insufficient. The bearer becomes aware, in a low-level persistent way, that they want more. This is not mechanical yet — it is a mood, an orientation.
- After two months: The bearer must succeed on a DC 14 Wisdom saving throw each week or use the Contact Drain on someone they are close to (ally, loved one, trusted companion) without intending to. They act casually, perhaps touching someone's hand, lingering in a hug, without fully acknowledging why.
- After four months: DC increases to 18. The bearer has difficulty spending time around people without feeling the drain opportunity. They begin to find reasons to touch people.
- After six months: The Contact Drain is always active, automatically activating on all extended physical contact whether the bearer wants it to or not.

**Why People Use It Anyway.** The persistent healing is genuinely valuable. The increased Constitution is genuinely valuable. The cost starts slowly, is not obviously the enhancement's fault at first, and by the time it is clearly a problem, the dependency has set in and removal means losing benefits that have become, functionally, part of how the bearer operates. There is also a specific and awful population of users who know exactly what the Hunger Wire does and consider it a feature.

## Where Found

Sold under deceptive names by a Wire Market black-market affiliate in the Second Reach. The Wire Market officially does not produce or endorse it. The affiliate officially does not exist. Both of these statements are technically true in the ways that matter for liability.
