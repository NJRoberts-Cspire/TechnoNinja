# Resonant Items
*From minor flickers of resonant metal to legendary items that reshape the people who use them. Seventy items across five tiers.*

---

> "Resonant metal does not contain magic. It contains memory. Every object made of resonant metal has been made by someone, used by someone, and carried through events — and the metal retains something of all of it. The difference between a common resonant item and a legendary one is how much of that memory is still active."
>
> — Wire Market Research Division, internal circulation, "On the Nature of Resonant Persistence," Year 47

### Burnstone
*Resonant Item | Common*

A smooth resonant-metal stone with an internal heating element — body-heat powered, it warms to a comfortable temperature and can be adjusted. Ashlands standard.
