# Information and Documents
*Maps, encoded files, Wire Market contracts, Iron Sutra texts, and access tokens. In Tesshari, what you know is often worth more than what you carry.*

---

> "The most dangerous thing in this empire is not a weapon. It is a document. Weapons end one situation. Documents restructure all the situations that follow."
>
> — Wire Market Senior Analyst Kave-Reth, internal memo, distribution restricted

## Maps and Navigation Items

---

### The Ashlands Truewall Survey
*Map | Rare*

A resonant-thread chart of the Ashlands' eastern perimeter, compiled over six years by a Craftbound surveyor named Osi-Thenn who went back each year to update it. The current edition shows three safe passes through the Truewall range, thirteen marked Engine Scar anomaly zones, eight survivable water sources, and one notation that reads "DO NOT enter the area marked with the red spiral. I am not explaining this." The red spiral is in the center of the map. Osi-Thenn returned from his sixth survey. He did not explain the red spiral.

**Narrative Function:** Provides authoritative navigation of the Ashlands eastern region. All navigation Utility cards in the surveyed area gain +3 to their effect. The three marked safe passes are accurate — using them removes all random contamination exposure from standard Ashlands travel. The thirteen anomaly zones, if avoided as marked, prevent automatic hazard encounters. The red spiral area: the GM has a plan for what is there.

**Cost:** 500 RM (Osi-Thenn sells these; he is still going back each year)

---

### Hollow Author's Last Known Movements
*Map/Intelligence Document | Very Rare*

A compiled intelligence report from a Wire Market analyst, itself a primary source of significant value — the report traces the Hollow Author's confirmed and suspected movements over eighteen months, cross-referencing witness accounts (anonymous), environmental data, and Wire Craft signal traces. The Hollow Author's location is documented at 22 specific points. The pattern, if analyzed, reveals something about the Author's operational priorities.

**Narrative Function:** A character who studies this document for 1 hour and succeeds on a SIGNAL check (target 5) identifies the operational pattern — they understand what locations the Hollow Author prioritizes and can make an educated prediction about where the Author would be at a given time or event type. All Utility cards to locate or predict the Hollow Author's position gain +3 to their effect.

**Rider:** The document itself is valuable. The Hollow Author is aware it exists. Carrying it may be noticed.

**Cost:** Not for sale. Currently in the possession of a Wire Market analyst who does not know she is in danger.

### Reach Seven Approach Charts
*Map | Uncommon*

Official cartography of the territory surrounding the Seventh Convergence's advance edge — produced by the Wire Market's survey division and marked "For Authorized Distribution Only," a classification they enforce through embarrassment rather than mechanism. Shows the confirmed Convergence boundary lines, three disputed boundary lines, and the evacuation routes the Wire Market officially recommends.

**Narrative Function:** All navigation Utility cards in areas bordering the Convergence territory gain +2 to their effect when using this chart. The evacuation routes, if followed, are reliable — they were planned with Wire Market infrastructure access in mind. Characters following the routes have access to one Wire Market supply cache per route segment (each cache contains 1d4 Wire Market Standard Healing Packs and 1d6 Ration Packs).

**Cost:** 80 RM (technically restricted; available without restriction from anyone who has one)

### Signal-Thread Map (Resonant Navigation)
*Map | Uncommon*

Not a paper or crystal map — a web of resonant threads stretched across a frame, each thread representing a Wire Market signal route between nodes. The threads carry actual signal; touching a junction pulses the navigator's hand with the distance, direction, and traffic status of the connected route. A living navigation system.

**Narrative Function:** Covers one Reach (purchaser's choice). While held, all Wire Market signal routes within that Reach are known to the bearer — real-time network status, fastest routes between any two points, and current traffic conditions. Navigation Utility cards in the covered Reach automatically succeed. The map self-updates through the Wire Market network when in a signal-connected area.

**Cost:** 300 RM per Reach coverage (Wire Market open catalog)

---

### Voided Pilgrim's Trail Record
*Map | Common*

A hand-drawn resonant-thread map made by a Voided pilgrim who walked a specific path through multiple Reaches without using Wire Market infrastructure. The map marks: non-Wire Market water sources, sympathetic communities (unnamed, marked by symbol), non-monitored border crossings, and rest sites. Inaccurate in places (the pilgrim made mistakes and noted them). Invaluable for off-grid travel.

**Narrative Function:** When navigating by this map along the marked routes, the bearer can travel between marked points without Wire Market network contact or infrastructure use. The routes avoid Wire Market checkpoints (noted where known). Navigation Utility cards along marked routes gain +2 to their effect. The map does not include any area south of the Ashlands border line.

**Cost:** 15 RM (available from Voided community contacts or found in pilgrim rest-sites)

## Encoded Documents and Blackmail Material

---

### The Broker's Burden (Financial Record)
*Encoded Document | Rare*

A resonant-crystal record containing seven years of financial transactions between a Wire Market senior broker and an unnamed Ashlands supplier — transactions that, if made public, would confirm the Wire Market's active commercial relationship with Ashlands materials smuggling. The broker does not know this record exists. Someone does. The person who does has been keeping it.

**Narrative Function:** Actionable blackmail against a named Wire Market senior broker (GM fills in name for campaign use). Possession provides leverage: the broker will perform one significant favor per document-viewing (showing them proof exists) before deciding the cost-benefit analysis of having the document destroyed. After three viewings, they make that calculation. The document also has legal value — the right authority would pay 5,000–20,000 RM for it.

**Social Rider:** All VEIL-based social cards against the targeted broker gain +4 to their effect while this document is held. The broker knows you have it.

**Cost:** Not available commercially. Whoever has it knows its value and is keeping it for a reason.

---

### The Heir's Confession
*Encoded Document | Uncommon*

A personal resonant recording made by the heir to a Reach lordship and deposited with a neutral party — a confession of an act that would void their inheritance claim if disclosed. The neutral party died. The recording passed through several hands. The heir is now the Reach lord, and the recording is somewhere in the Third Reach's gray market.

**Narrative Function:** Social leverage over one Reach lord (GM identifies which one for their campaign). The Reach lord is aware this recording exists and will make significant concessions (safe passage, political favors, payment) to have it returned without disclosure. If disclosed to their rivals or the Wire Market, the political consequences are severe and constitute a plot-relevant event. The recording is encoded — a SIGNAL check target 4 to decode it.

**Social Rider:** All VEIL-based social cards against the targeted Reach lord gain +3 to their effect while this document is held.

**Cost:** 800 RM on the gray market (the seller does not know what it contains; they know it is worth something to someone)

---

### The Null-Screen Protocol (Wire Market Internal)
*Encoded Document | Very Rare*

An internal Wire Market document detailing a protocol they do not officially use: the Null-Screen Protocol, which provides a procedure for covertly suppressing information about specific individuals from all Wire Market network records. The Protocol has been used. The document lists instances. Several of the suppressed individuals are people who were publicly declared dead. Several are people who were publicly declared to never have existed.

**Narrative Function:** Devastating to the Wire Market's credibility if disclosed publicly — it confirms that the Wire Market actively manipulates the identity records it serves as the authoritative source for. Possession creates extreme leverage over Wire Market senior leadership. The Wire Market will pursue recovery through any means they judge proportionate, starting with incentives and not stopping there. A Wire Craft practitioner studying it (SIGNAL check target 6, 1 week of study) can learn to apply the suppression protocol to one individual's records.

**Social Rider:** All VEIL and SIGNAL cards against Wire Market senior leadership gain +5 to their effect while this document is held. The Wire Market's response to leverage use escalates after each use.

**Cost:** Not for sale. The Wire Market will pay 50,000 RM for its return and claim they are offering this for completeness of public record.

### The Seventh Register's Missing Page
*Encoded Document | Rare*

A single resonant-thread page from a government ledger — specifically, the Seventh Reach's resource allocation register, from a period predating the Seventh Convergence. The page records the diversion of substantial resonant-metal reserves from civilian infrastructure to an unnamed project. The project name is redacted except for the first word: "The." The redaction was done after the page was printed. Someone knew where to look.

**Narrative Function:** Evidence of pre-Convergence resource manipulation in the Seventh Reach — relevant to investigations of why the Seventh Reach's collapse was so total and so fast. A scholar or investigator who studies this document and succeeds on a SIGNAL check (target 5) can determine the scope of the diverted resources and make an educated inference about "The [redacted]" project. This information has significant campaign implications (GM determines the specific revelation).

**Cost:** 400 RM to the right buyer; the wrong buyer would not know what they have

---

### Vaen's Private Correspondence (Intercepted)
*Encoded Document | Very Rare*

A set of three intercepted messages between Vaen (the spiritual Claimant) and an unnamed correspondent — not their publicly known communications, but private ones. Two of the messages are philosophical. One is not. The one that is not is the reason this correspondence has value.

**Narrative Function:** The non-philosophical message, decoded (SIGNAL check target 5), reveals information about Vaen's actual intentions regarding the Seventh Convergence — intentions that do not perfectly align with their public spiritual mission. The nature of the revelation is GM-determined, but should complicate the players' assessment of Vaen as a figure. The document also provides leverage against Vaen, though Vaen's response to blackmail attempts will be characteristically indirect.

**Social Rider:** All VEIL-based social cards against Vaen gain +3 to their effect while this document is held — and Vaen knows it exists.

**Cost:** Not commercially available. In the possession of a Wire Market intelligence analyst who intercepted it and is trying to decide what to do with it.

## Wire Market Contracts and Credentials

---

### Field Operative ID (Wire Market)
*Wire Market Credential | Uncommon*

A Wire Market-issued identity credential for field operatives — a resonant card with the bearer's identity, authorization level, and a field operative designation. Provides access to standard Wire Market facilities and resources.

**Narrative Function:** The bearer is identified as a Wire Market field operative (junior designation). Provides: access to Wire Market standard facilities without credential challenge; 20% discount on Wire Market open catalog purchases; right to request information from Wire Market information desks (general-availability information only). The ID is trackable — the Wire Market knows where it was last used.

**Social Rider:** All VEIL-based social cards with Wire Market personnel gain +2 to their effect. The bearer is considered Wire Market-employed, which has social and legal implications throughout the Reaches.

**Cost:** Not for sale. Issued only. Available on the black market at 1,500 RM; forgery quality varies significantly.

---

### Freelancer Contract (Wire Market Standard)
*Wire Market Contract | Common*

A standard Wire Market engagement contract for freelance work — the Wire Market's entry-level agreement for non-staff operators. Provides certain protections and certain obligations. The obligations are written in very small signal-script on the back of the contract.

**Narrative Function:** Establishes the bearer as a Wire Market-authorized freelancer. Benefits: Wire Market legal protection in Reach territory (not absolute; the Wire Market can withdraw protection); access to one Wire Market supply cache per mission (pre-arranged). Obligations: 15% of all mission-related compensation to the Wire Market; disclosure of significant intelligence findings; non-competition with direct Wire Market interests. The obligations are not always enforced. They are always retained.

**Cost:** Free to sign. The Wire Market does not charge to take on new freelancers. They find other ways to recoup.

---

### Master Installer Certification (Wire Craft)
*Wire Market Credential | Rare*

A Wire Market-issued certification confirming the bearer is a qualified Master Wire Craft Installer — authorized to perform enhancements of any complexity, install experimental enhancements, and certify other installers. The actual training takes years. The certification itself, forged, is worth a significant amount.

**Narrative Function:** The bearer (or someone presenting it) is treated as a Master Installer for Wire Market purposes — they can perform any installation without Wire Market audit, access Wire Market-restricted medical facilities for installation work, and purchase restricted enhancement components without additional clearance.

**Mechanical Rider:** Legitimately earned: all enhancement installation Utility cards gain +3 to their effect. Forged: all the social benefits, none of the skill.

**Cost:** Not for direct sale. Legitimate certification requires years of training and a Wire Market assessment. Forged certifications: 4,000 RM on the black market. Quality varies; the Wire Market's verification system is thorough.

---

### Network Access Token (Tier Two)
*Wire Market Credential | Uncommon*

A resonant crystal that, when interfaced with a Wire Market network node, grants Tier Two network access — above public access, below operative access. Tier Two allows access to non-public information databases, Restricted Catalog browsing (purchasing requires separate clearance), and basic personnel records.

**Narrative Function:** The bearer can access the Wire Market's Tier Two network from any network node. This provides: access to non-public records (commercial, legal, most personnel records except classified); access to the Restricted Catalog (browse only; Tier Two cannot purchase restricted items); ability to submit information requests answered in 1d4 days. The Token is traceable — the Wire Market logs every Tier Two access.

**Cost:** 200 RM (Wire Market sells Tier Two tokens to registered commercial clients; the registration process is the real barrier)

---

### Travel Warrant (Cross-Reach)
*Wire Market Credential | Common*

A Wire Market-issued travel document authorizing movement between specified Reaches — the Wire Market facilitates cross-Reach travel and charges for the facilitation. The Warrant is processed through their network and recognized by most Reach border checkpoints.

**Narrative Function:** The bearer (and up to 4 named companions on the document) can cross specified Reach borders without standard immigration challenge. Does not provide access to restricted zones within the Reach. Cross-Reach travel typically takes 1d4 days of administrative processing; the Wire Market can expedite for a fee.

**Cost:** 25 RM per Reach-crossing pair (1 originating Reach, 1 destination Reach). Expedited processing: 50 RM additional.

## Iron Sutra Texts and Access Tokens

---

### The Complete Sutra of the Iron Afterlife
*Iron Sutra Text | Uncommon*

The full doctrinal text of the Iron Sutra — not a summary, not an excerpt, but the complete text as maintained by the Fifth Reach's primary temple. Thousands of pages of resonant-crystal recording, in a carrying case with a convenient interface crystal. Heavy. Comprehensive. The Iron Sutra temple communities believe having one is a blessing. The Wire Market believes having one means you are reliably predictable.

**Narrative Function:** The bearer has access to the complete Iron Sutra doctrine. They can answer any question about Iron Sutra belief, practice, and protocol with authority. Iron Sutra practitioners treat the bearer as a scholar. All RESONANCE-based Utility cards related to Iron Sutra rituals and Afterlife protocols gain +2 to their effect when the Sutra is consulted.

**Cost:** 150 RM (temple edition); 50 RM (street edition, likely abridged or inaccurate — SIGNAL check target 3 to identify the difference)

---

### Iron Afterlife Access Crystal (Public)
*Iron Sutra Access Token | Common*

A resonant crystal pre-tuned to the Iron Afterlife's public communication layer — the outermost ring of the digital spirit world, where general-access broadcasts are transmitted. Allows any user with a Wire Craft interface to receive Iron Afterlife public broadcasts and post to the public record.

**Narrative Function:** The bearer can receive and post to the Iron Afterlife's public communication layer — the general resonant broadcast accessible to anyone with the right crystal. They can receive announcements, Afterlife echo broadcasts, and general spiritual transmissions from deceased individuals who chose to post publicly. They cannot contact specific individuals through this crystal.

**Cost:** 15 RM (temples often give these away; the Wire Market considers their distribution a public utility)

---

### Prior Ascended Location Record (Temple Edition)
*Iron Sutra Text | Rare*

A temple-maintained record of all confirmed Prior Ascended site locations — places where the Ascended walked, worked, or departed from the physical world. Compiled over four hundred years of temple scholarship. The locations are real; the details about each site are the product of four hundred years of scholarship with varying levels of quality control.

**Narrative Function:** The bearer knows the location of all 23 confirmed Prior Ascended sites in Tesshari. At each site, all History and RESONANCE-based Utility cards related to the Ascended gain +3 to their effect. The record also notes the resonant properties of each site and lists each site's current status (many are controlled by one of the Three Claimants or the Wire Market; the record is honest about this).

**Cost:** 600 RM (temple price); the Wire Market buys these regularly and would prefer there were fewer of them available

---

### Temple Pass (Iron Sutra Major)
*Iron Sutra Access Token | Uncommon*

A resonant token issued by a major Iron Sutra temple that grants the bearer access to that temple's full facilities: the library archives, the Afterlife communication chambers, the consecrated meditation halls, and the clinical installation suite where Iron Sutra priests perform blessed cybernetic work.

**Narrative Function:** The bearer has unrestricted access to the issuing temple's facilities. They can use: the Iron Afterlife communication chamber (private sessions); the full doctrinal archive; the meditation halls (all RESONANCE-based ritual card actions cost 0 AP here); and the installation suite (all enhancement installation Utility cards performed by temple staff gain +3 to their effect). The token is temple-specific and does not transfer to other temples without a letter of introduction.

**Cost:** 50 RM one-time fee for temple registration; then 30 RM annual maintenance

---

### Voided Path Witness Record
*Iron Sutra Text | Common*

A testimonial document — a resonant-crystal record of a Voided pilgrim's journey, in their own words and experience, from the decision to surrender enhancements to the completion of a significant pilgrimage. These are produced by the Iron Sutra as doctrinal inspiration, but the Wire Market regards them with interest because the Voided path produces individuals who are invisible to standard monitoring and the testimonials sometimes reveal why people chose invisibility.

**Narrative Function:** The bearer has a first-hand account of the Voided experience — useful for understanding Voided communities. All VEIL-based social cards when dealing with Voided individuals gain +2 to their effect. The document can also serve as a social credential with Voided communities — showing it demonstrates respect for the tradition.

**Cost:** 8 RM (Iron Sutra temples sell them as inspirational literature); 40 RM to Wire Market intelligence buyers who want the specific details
