## Wire Market Tools and Technology

---

### Analysis Frame (Portable Scanning Unit)
*Wire Market Tool | Uncommon*

A folding frame of resonant metal the size of a large book, with six scanning nodes on articulated arms. The nodes project a resonant scan field through any object placed within the frame — delivering a detailed compositional and structural analysis. Standard Wire Market field equipment for material verification.

**Effect:** Any object placed within the Analysis Frame's scan field is fully analyzed in 5 minutes: material composition, resonant signature, Wire Market manufacturing stamps, structural integrity, and any concealed internal components. Identifies any Wire Market-manufactured items by origin facility and production date. Also functions as a basic medical scanner assessing an individual's enhancement configuration and current HP tier.

**Cost:** 800 RM

---

### Breach Kit (Entry Specialist)
*Wire Market Tool | Uncommon*

A compact set of tools in a wire-silk carrying case: resonant lock-pick set (12 standard probe types), a micro-signal bypass card, a friction cutter for physical locks, and a fiber-resonant viewer for seeing through doors. Wire Market issue for field operatives.

**Effect:** Contains tools for bypassing locks — all SIGNAL and EDGE checks for bypassing resonant and signal locks gain +3; physical locks gain +1. The fiber viewer allows visual inspection of what is directly behind a door or wall (up to 2 inches thick) as a free action. The micro-signal bypass card grants one automatic success on a Wire Market Standard access system check per day.

**Cost:** 450 RM

---

### Compact Survey Drone
*Wire Market Tool | Rare*

A drone the size of a large fist — six resonant wings, a signal-relay body, and a sensor package including visual, thermal, and resonant scanning. Controlled via a paired interface card. Range: 300 feet from operator.

**Effect:** Deploy as a free action; direct as a Tier 0 card action each turn. The drone has Stealth +6 equivalent, 5 HP, and Defense Value 2. Its sensors relay in real-time: visual at normal quality; thermal (sees body heat through thin walls); resonant scanning (detects active enhancements within 30 feet of the drone). Flight speed is fast — it can cross a standard battlefield in one turn.

**Cost:** 2,500 RM

---

### Deadkey Override (Universal Access Tool)
*Wire Market Tool | Rare*

A Wire Market-issued master access tool — a physical key and signal emitter combination that can access Wire Market-standard security systems at junior-operative level. Not unlimited access; not no access. Somewhere specific.

**Effect:** Grants automatic access to Wire Market Standard Tier security systems (most basic Wire Market-affiliated buildings, common-floor access, standard locks). Does not function on Restricted or higher-tier systems. The Deadkey's use is logged — the Wire Market knows when and where it was used. Any Deadkey in the field is an issued or stolen one, and the Wire Market tracks both categories.

**Cost:** Not for general sale. Issued to Wire Market operatives. Available on the black market at approximately 3,000 RM.

---

### Interface Gloves
*Wire Market Tool | Common*

Wire-silk gloves with resonant contact points at the fingertips, palm center, and wrist. Allow tactile Wire Craft interface with signal systems, enhancement ports, and data storage. Required for Wire Craft practitioners who want to work without direct skin contact.

**Effect:** Allow Wire Craft Utility card actions through the gloves (normally Wire Craft requires direct skin contact with resonant surfaces). They also prevent accidental triggering of Wire Craft systems through casual contact. Wire Craft checks made through the gloves take -1 to their effect (the extra layer reduces sensitivity slightly).

**Cost:** 35 RM

---

### Portable Signal Amplifier
*Wire Market Tool | Uncommon*

A fist-sized resonant antenna that boosts the signal strength of Wire Craft communications and tools in its vicinity. Used in areas where the Wire Market's network signal is degraded — deep Ashlands territory, signal-disrupted zones, underground facilities.

**Effect:** While active (deploy as a free action, lasts 8 hours), all Wire Craft Utility and Signal card actions made within 100 feet gain +2 to their effect. Long-Wire Communicators within range extend their effective range by 50%. In Signal Cage disruption fields, the Amplifier reduces the disruption radius by 30 feet against the Amplifier's users.

**Cost:** 600 RM

---

### Resonant Lockbox (Secure Storage)
*Wire Market Tool | Uncommon*

A small box in reinforced resonant metal with a signal-key lock. The lock requires a specific Echomind signature to open (set during configuration) and cannot be opened by physical means (Defense Value equivalent: 30; 50 HP before destruction). Wire Market personnel records can reset the lock if the original keyholder is dead.

**Effect:** Stores contents securely. Only the configured Echomind can open it; on a wrong signal, it registers an unauthorized-access attempt. After three failed access attempts, it triggers a silent alert to the Wire Market's nearest monitoring station (if the party has this, the Wire Market knows they have it). Physical destruction requires dealing 50 HP of damage at a Defense Value of 30 — contents are typically damaged in the process.

**Cost:** 150 RM

---

### Signal Tap (Network Monitoring)
*Wire Market Tool | Rare*

A passive monitoring device installed on a Wire Market network node or signal conduit — records all traffic through that node for up to one week. The Wire Market uses these for network monitoring. People use these to find out what the Wire Market is monitoring.

**Effect:** When physically attached to an active signal conduit (EDGE check target 4 to attach without notice; requires access to the conduit), records all signal traffic through that conduit for 7 days. A Wire Craft practitioner can review the recording to identify specific communications (SIGNAL check target 3 per communication type sought). Detection: SIGNAL check target 5 on active scanning.

**Cost:** 350 RM

---

### Tesshari Tool Roll (Standard Field Kit)
*Wire Market Tool | Common*

A wire-silk roll containing the standard equipment for field-grade Wire Craft maintenance: assorted probe sizes, calibration tools, flux compound applicator, signal tester, and a small emergency Forge-Wire spool. Not glamorous. Keeps everything else working.

**Effect:** Contains all tools required for standard cybernetic enhancement maintenance. All enhancement-related Utility card actions gain +1 to their effect. The field kit does not expire; individual components need replacement as used.

**Cost:** 45 RM

---

### Wire-Trace Reader
*Wire Market Tool | Common*

A hand-held scanning wand that reads resonant signatures from objects and surfaces. Lower-fidelity than the Analysis Frame — this is the field version, not the laboratory version.

**Effect:** A 1-minute scan of any object or surface provides its resonant signature and origin type (Wire Market standard, Ashlands origin, Prior Ascended, etc.). Identifies Wire Market-manufactured items by catalog type (SIGNAL check target 4 for model specifics). Useful for quick checks rather than thorough analysis.

**Cost:** 80 RM

## Sutensai and Religious Items

---

### Afterlife Registry Key
*Iron Sutra Religious Item | Uncommon*

A resonant crystal programmed with access codes to the Iron Afterlife's archived records — not the complete archive (that is only accessible from within the Afterlife), but the public registry of Echomind arrivals. Useful for confirming whether a specific person has died and entered the Afterlife.

**Effect:** A Wire Craft practitioner or Iron Sutra priest using the Registry Key can query the Iron Afterlife's public arrival record. They can confirm whether a specific named individual has died and their Echomind has entered the Afterlife (within the last 5 years — older records require deeper access). They cannot access the individual's Afterlife location or communicate with them through the Key alone.

**Cost:** 200 RM (available at major Iron Sutra temples)

---

### Devotional Resonant Beads
*Iron Sutra Religious Item | Common*

A string of 108 resonant-metal beads, each individually attuned during a consecration ceremony. Used in Iron Sutra meditation practice for counting repetitions of the Sutra's core mantras. The resonant alignment of the beads, accumulated from years of devotional use, develops mild properties.

**Effect:** When held and core mantras are recited during a short rest, the bearer may reroll one HP restoration result from the rest. Additionally, after completing 10 minutes of mantra recitation with the beads, the bearer's next Defense card against a RESONANCE or Echomind-targeting effect gains +3 to its effect.

**Cost:** 15 RM (new); 50–200 RM for beads with significant devotional history

---

### Echomind Lantern (Iron Sutra)
*Iron Sutra Religious Item | Uncommon*

A resonant lantern in Iron Sutra iconographic style — the Ascended's symbol engraved on the base, the Afterlife glyph on the housing. The lantern's light is tuned to the Iron Afterlife spectrum: in standard environments, it emits normal light; in areas of Afterlife resonance, it adjusts to show Afterlife presence.

**Effect:** Produces light (30-foot radius). In areas of Iron Afterlife presence, the light shifts to a deep violet and fluctuates near Afterlife manifestations — passive Afterlife presence detection with no card required. Iron Sutra practitioners using the lantern during ritual have their RESONANCE-based Utility cards gain +2 to their effect. The lantern never runs out of fuel (resonant-metal burning element).

**Cost:** 120 RM

---

### Flowing Script Prayer Board
*Iron Sutra Religious Item | Uncommon*

A flat resonant plate that displays continuously scrolling Iron Sutra scripture — the script is signal-written and self-renewing, cycling through the complete Sutra text approximately once per hour. Used in temple settings and by traveling priests who cannot carry physical volumes.

**Effect:** Any creature that can read Sutra script and spends 10 minutes with the Prayer Board gains +2 to all Defense cards against Fear and Echomind-affecting Control cards for 1 hour. Iron Sutra practitioners using the Board as their ritual focus have the AP cost of RESONANCE ritual card actions reduced by 1 (minimum 1 AP).

**Cost:** 250 RM

---

### Iron Sutra Monk's Tool Belt
*Iron Sutra Religious Item | Common*

A practical item for traveling Iron Sutra practitioners: a wire-silk belt with small pouches for ritual components, a resonant crystal holder for the basic Sutra texts, and a small auto-incense emitter that can be manually triggered for brief meditation moments in the field.

**Effect:** All Iron Sutra ritual components stored in the belt can be retrieved as a free action (normally a Tier 0 card action). The auto-incense emitter can be triggered as a free action for a 1-round resonant atmosphere — the bearer gains +2 to the next RESONANCE-based Utility card they play this round.

**Cost:** 40 RM

---

### Relic-Case (Sacred Object Transport)
*Iron Sutra Religious Item | Common*

A resonant-lined case for transporting sacred objects — particularly resonant items with Afterlife residue or Prior Ascended fragments. The lining prevents resonant contamination of the surrounding environment and prevents the contents from accumulating additional contamination from outside.

**Effect:** Contains any resonant object's passive card-modifying aura within the case — an object that would normally affect creatures within 10 feet (passive resonant effects) has no effect while cased. Prevents resonant degradation of the contents. Standard Iron Sutra practice for transporting anything with significant resonant charge.

**Cost:** 35 RM

---

### Resonant Altar Cloth
*Iron Sutra Religious Item | Common*

A wire-silk cloth used to designate a temporary sacred space. The cloth's resonant weave creates a mild consecrated field when spread on a flat surface with the proper Sutra markings.

**Effect:** When spread and the dedication ritual is spoken (1 minute): creates a 5-foot consecrated area for 1 hour. In this area, RESONANCE-based ritual card actions cost 0 AP; Iron Afterlife communication Utility cards gain +2 to their effect; and any creature resting here restores 1 additional HP per HP recovery die spent during a short rest.

**Cost:** 20 RM

---

### Sutensai's Register (Appointment Record)
*Iron Sutra Religious Item | Uncommon*

A resonant-crystal record of appointments between specific individuals and the Iron Afterlife — essentially an appointment book for the dead. Iron Sutra priests maintain these for clients who have made arrangements for Afterlife contact at scheduled times. Having one is the social equivalent of being known to the local temple community.

**Effect:** Provides the bearer with standing access to scheduled Iron Afterlife contact sessions at the nearest Iron Sutra temple — once per week, the bearer can participate in a formal contact session facilitated by a temple priest (RESONANCE check target 3 with assistance). Additionally, the Register identifies the bearer as a registered Iron Sutra community member — VEIL-based social cards with devout practitioners gain +2 to their effect.

**Cost:** 30 RM per year subscription; initial registration 50 RM

---

### The Ascending Primer (Iron Sutra Study Text)
*Iron Sutra Religious Item | Common*

A resonant-crystal recording of the basic Iron Sutra doctrine and practice — the entry-level doctrinal text given to new practitioners. The crystal plays the Sutra's core teachings through a simple resonant interface (ear-touch on the crystal's interface point).

**Effect:** A character who studies the Primer for 8 hours gains familiarity with Iron Sutra doctrine sufficient to pass social scrutiny as a practitioner. They can attempt Iron Sutra-specific Utility card actions without the unfamiliar-tradition penalty. This does not grant Wire Craft or spiritual card abilities — only doctrinal knowledge.

**Cost:** 10 RM (temple price); 6 RM (street vendor, likely a copy)

---

### Voided Pilgrim's Staff
*Iron Sutra Religious Item | Common*

A wooden staff (rare in Tesshari, making it a deliberate statement) with minimal resonant metal content — a small node at the top and a ferrule at the base, just enough to function as a social marker of the Voided path. The limitation is the point.

**Effect:** Functions as a melee weapon — all Melee Attack cards played with this staff deal bludgeoning damage. Carries no enhancement interfaces. A Voided character wielding this staff is immediately recognizable as following the Voided path; Iron Sutra practitioners offer hospitality (free lodging and food at Iron Sutra temples). Non-Voided characters who carry this staff are presumed to be making a philosophical statement, which generates conversation.

**Cost:** 12 RM

## General Adventuring Equipment (Tesshari-Flavored)

---

### Ashlands Survival Kit
*General Equipment | Common*

A compact pack with the essential Ashlands field kit: wire-silk filtration mask (standard particulate filter), sealed water container (resonant-lined to prevent contamination), Ashlands-grade navigation crystal (adjusts for the Ashlands' distorted magnetic field), emergency signal beacon (single use), and three days of caloric-dense field rations.

**Effect:** Provides: all Defense card actions against standard Ashlands contamination gain +3 for 3 days of use; 1 gallon of safe water per day from the sealed container; all navigation Utility cards in Ashlands territory gain +2 to their effect; the emergency beacon, when activated, is detectable by Wire Craft scanning within 5 miles for 6 hours (generates a Wire Market response in monitored areas within 2d6 hours).

**Cost:** 35 RM

---

### Climbing Frame (Enhanced Harness)
*General Equipment | Common*

A wire-silk climbing harness with integrated resonant anchor bolts and auto-tensioning wire-lash reels. Makes ascending and descending structures significantly more reliable.

**Effect:** All Athletics-based Utility cards for climbing gain +3 to their effect. The auto-tensioning system provides a belay function — if the bearer falls while harnessed and attached to an anchor, they stop falling after 10 feet (the tensioner catches the fall). Four anchor bolts included; replacement bolts 2 RM each.

**Cost:** 25 RM

---

### Compact Tent (Wire-Frame)
*General Equipment | Common*

A tent that assembles from a wire-silk sheet and a set of resonant-metal poles that form the frame. The frame self-assembles when the anchor-bolts are set (free action to set, free action to assemble). Packs very small. Provides weatherproofing and mild signal dampening.

**Effect:** Provides weatherproofing and modest resonant-signal dampening — Wire Craft scanning cannot precisely locate someone inside (detects presence in the tent but not exact position within it). Assembly: 2 free actions.

**Cost:** 20 RM

---

### Emergency Flare (Signal)
*General Equipment | Common*

A resonant flare that burns in both visible and signal spectrum — a combined visual/Wire Craft distress signal. Visible as a bright light (1 mile in clear conditions); detectable as a resonant signal burst at up to 5 miles by anyone with Wire Craft scanning capability.

**Effect:** Burns for 5 minutes. Visible light radius 100 feet. Signal detection within 5 miles by Wire Craft scanning. The Wire Market's monitoring network detects all activated flares in their coverage area — using one in a Wire Market-monitored area generates a response within 2d6 hours (helpful or inconvenient, depending on circumstances).

**Cost:** 5 RM each; 20 RM for 5-pack

---

### Field Pack (Resonant-Lined)
*General Equipment | Common*

A standard field pack with a resonant-metal interior lining that prevents the contents from being scanned by basic Wire Craft scanning equipment. Not a hardened case — a basic scanning countermeasure for the carrying of items you would prefer not to be identified.

**Effect:** Carries up to 50 pounds. Contents cannot be identified by basic Wire Craft scanning (handheld scanners, standard checkpoint scanners). Advanced equipment (Analysis Frame, high-tier scanning) can breach the lining with a SIGNAL check target 5.

**Cost:** 30 RM

---

### Grapple-Wire Launcher
*General Equipment | Common*

A compact launcher firing a grappling hook on 60 feet of resonant wire-silk — the hook carries a resonant anchor point that adheres to resonant surfaces. Essential equipment for navigating Tesshari's upper-Reach architecture and ruined structures.

**Effect:** Launches a grapple to any visible surface within 60 feet as a free action. EDGE check target 2 to hit a specific small target; automatic for large stable surfaces. The wire-silk line holds up to 400 pounds. Retracts via a crank in 1 minute or can be cut. Extra wire-silk spools for replacement: 8 RM per 60-foot spool.

**Cost:** 35 RM

---

### Ration Pack (Wire Market Standard)
*General Equipment | Common*

Compressed nutrient blocks in Wire Market-standard caloric density — not pleasant, but complete. Provides all required macronutrients and several resonant-supplement micronutrients. Each block is one day's food requirement.

**Effect:** One block provides a full day's nutritional requirement including basic resonant-supplement requirements for enhanced individuals. Does not prevent Overheat from enhancement overuse, but prevents exhaustion from food deprivation. The taste is described as "neutral" by people who are being generous about it.

**Cost:** 2 RM per day; 12 RM per week's supply

---

### Resonant Rope (50 ft.)
*General Equipment | Common*

Wire-silk rope with a resonant-metal weave throughout — stronger than non-resonant rope, and resistant to being cut by standard blades. The resonant weave also gives it a slight stiffness that makes it useful as a push-rod in addition to a pull-cord.

**Effect:** 50 feet, holds up to 2,000 pounds. Cannot be cut by standard blades (Defense Value 10 against cutting). Can be rigidified up to 5 feet as a free action (toggle) — the wire-silk stiffens, usable as a prop, brace, or lever.

**Cost:** 15 RM

---

### Signal Flare (Directed)
*General Equipment | Common*

Unlike the standard emergency flare, this one fires in a specific direction — a small launcher, 20-foot range, delivering a burst of signal and light in a concentrated direction. Used for coordinating allies across line-of-sight distances when silence is required.

**Effect:** Sends a directed signal burst in a visible beam (200 feet in clear conditions). A specific pre-coded signal can be programmed (1 minute, SIGNAL check target 2 to program); the recipient with a Wire Craft device recognizes the coded burst. As an improvised weapon: 3 fire damage, target gains Stagger 1 if it hits the face (FRAME check target 2 or Staggered for 1 round).

**Cost:** 3 RM each; 15 RM per 6-pack

---

### Wire-Silk Blanket
*General Equipment | Common*

A blanket of heavy wire-silk that provides thermal regulation — resonant threads in the weave respond to the bearer's body heat and calibrate insulation accordingly. Warm in cold; breathable in heat.

**Effect:** Prevents all non-resonant environmental cold and heat damage from temperature extremes. The bearer cannot suffer the effects of extreme cold or extreme heat environments (Ashlands heat, mountain cold) while wrapped in the blanket. Also, it's comfortable. In Tesshari, comfort is not something to take for granted.

**Cost:** 10 RM
